This page contains the complete bill of materials for building a standard OSSM with the 3030 extrusion stand.

<Info>
For PitClamp-specific hardware, see the [PitClamp BOM](/ossm/Hardware/standard-printed-parts/pitclamp/BOM).
</Info>

## Actuator

The actuator is the core linear motion assembly of the OSSM.

### Motor

| Quantity | Part |
|----------|------|
| 1 | 57AIM30 Gold Motor |

### Linear Motion

| Quantity | Part |
|----------|------|
| 1 | MGN12H Linear Rail (350mm suggested) |

<Info>
Calculate your rail length using this formula: **desired maximum stroke + 180mm**. A 350mm rail is suggested for most builds.
</Info>

### Belt and Pulley

| Quantity | Part |
|----------|------|
| 1 | GT2 20T Pulley (8mm bore, 10mm width) |
| 500mm | GT2 Timing Belt (10mm width) |

### Bearings

| Quantity | Part |
|----------|------|
| 6 | MR115-2RS 5x11x4mm Ball Bearing |

## Stand (3030 Extrusion)

The stand provides a stable, adjustable mounting platform for the OSSM.

### Aluminum Extrusion

| Quantity | Length | Profile |
|----------|--------|---------|
| 2 | 12" | 3030 |
| 1 | 18" | 3030 |
| 1 | 19.5" | 3030 |

### Hardware - Bag 1

| Quantity | Part |
|----------|------|
| 14 | M6x12 Socket Cap Head Screw |
| 6 | M6x25 Socket Cap Head Screw |
| 16 | M6 Washer |
| 24 | M6 T-Nut (3030 profile) |

### Hardware - Bag 2

| Quantity | Part |
|----------|------|
| 2 | M6x25mm Handle |

### Hardware - Bag 3

| Quantity | Part |
|----------|------|
| 4 | 90 Degree 3030 Corner Bracket |
| 2 | Rubber Washer |
| 4 | Non-Slip Furniture Pad (Grippy Feet) |
| 6 | 3030 End Cap |

### Accessories

| Quantity | Part |
|----------|------|
| 1 | Sandbag |
