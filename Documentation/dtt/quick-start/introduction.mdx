## Set up in minutes

<Steps>
<Step title="Create your dashboard account (one time)">
Go to the registration guide and create your account. Confirm your email to finish setup.

<Check>
You can sign in at any time to see devices, templates, and results.
</Check>

</Step>

<Step title="Power on and find your Trainer ID">
Charge your Deepthroat Trainer, then turn it on. During boot, note the “Trainer ID: XXXXX” shown on screen.

<Tip>
If you miss it, restart the device to see the Trainer ID again.
</Tip>
</Step>

<Step title="Pair your trainer to the dashboard">
Sign in to the dashboard, open Settings → Devices, and enter your Trainer ID. Select Pair Device.

<Check>
Your trainer appears under My Devices and shows as paired.
</Check>

<Info>
If a device is already paired to someone else, have the original owner unpair it first or see the ownership policy in FAQs.
</Info>
</Step>

<Step title="Connect your trainer to Wi‑Fi">
Put the trainer in Settings (press and hold the button from the home screen) and connect to the temporary “Trainer Setup” network on your phone. Use the captive portal to choose your 2.4 GHz home network, enter the password, and save. The trainer will restart and connect.

<Warning>
The trainer connects only to 2.4 GHz Wi‑Fi. If your router combines 2.4/5 GHz under one name, create a separate 2.4 GHz SSID or use your router’s “IoT”/guest 2.4 GHz network.
</Warning>

<Check>
After restart, the display shows that Wi‑Fi is connected.
</Check>
</Step>

<Step title="Prepare your toy and workspace">
Choose a compatible toy and apply water‑based lubricant. Position the trainer on a stable surface. You can use your own dildo or R+D accessories.

<Tip>
See FAQs for toy compatibility and tips.
</Tip>
</Step>

<Step title="Start your first session">
From the dashboard, pick a training template or start with a simple freeform session, then fetch settings to your device and press Start on the trainer.

<Info>
You can also explore community templates and import one if your subscription tier allows.
</Info>
</Step>

<Step title="Review your results and progress">
After training, visit the dashboard to see grades and points. Track improvements, compare sessions, and view your leaderboard ranking.

<Check>
Results appear on your dashboard once the trainer is online and sync completes.
</Check>
</Step>
</Steps>

## Tips, safety, and common questions

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Do I need Wi‑Fi for every session?">
The trainer works best online so it can fetch the latest training and upload results. If you run a session while offline, results won’t upload until the trainer reconnects.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Partner features and competition">
Invite a partner to view your results and hold you accountable, and use points to compete on leaderboards. Learn how scoring works in the points guide.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Cleaning and care">
Wipe the trainer body with a soft microfiber cloth and a mild surface cleaner (avoid soaking). Clean toys according to their material guidelines. Always disconnect power before cleaning.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Troubleshooting pairing and Wi‑Fi">
- Can’t find your Trainer ID? Restart the device and check the boot screen.
- Already paired to someone else? Ask the original owner to unpair, or see the device ownership policy in FAQs.
- Wi‑Fi won’t connect? Ensure you’re on 2.4 GHz, verify the password, and move the trainer closer to the router.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
