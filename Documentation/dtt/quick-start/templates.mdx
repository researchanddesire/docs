---
title: "Shareable Templates"
description: "Discover, import, and share training templates created by the community"
---

Browse public training templates created by other users and import them into your collection from the Explore page in your dashboard.

## Find templates

Navigate to Deepthroat Trainer → Explore Templates in your dashboard. The Explore page lists public templates with creator info, difficulty, and descriptions so you can quickly find options that match your goals.

## Enable public sharing

Templates are private by default. When creating or editing a template, toggle Public Sharing to make it discoverable on the Explore page.

<Note>
When you enable Public Sharing, add a short, specific description so others understand what makes your template unique and when to use it.
</Note>

## Import a template

<Steps>
<Step title="Browse available templates">
Open the Explore Templates page and browse available options. Filter by difficulty or scan descriptions to find a good match.
</Step>

<Step title="Preview details">
Click <strong>View</strong> on any template to see segments, difficulty, and creator notes.
</Step>

<Step title="Import to your collection">
If you have an eligible subscription, click <strong>Use Template</strong> or <strong>Import</strong> to add it to your collection.

<Check>
After importing, the template appears in your Templates list as read‑only and is ready to assign to a toy.
</Check>
</Step>
</Steps>

## What happens after import

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Read‑only access">
Imported templates are read‑only. You can view settings and segments but cannot modify training segments or the template itself. This preserves the original author’s intent.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Toy assignment">
You can assign which of your toys the imported template uses.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Removing templates">
Remove an imported template from your collection any time by selecting <strong>Stop following</strong> or <strong>Remove</strong>. Removing an import does not affect the original public template.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Info>
You cannot import your own template.
</Info>

## Active template limits

You can have one active imported template at a time. This prevents conflicts between multiple imported templates. Mark your imported template as active or inactive in its settings.

## Privacy and moderation

- Templates are private by default.
- Enabling Public Sharing makes only the template and its public metadata (name, description, difficulty, segments) visible to authenticated users via the Explore page.
- Existing templates remain private after the feature release—sharing must be explicitly enabled by the owner.

<Tip>
To improve discoverability, use a descriptive title, choose an accurate difficulty rating, and write a concise summary that highlights who the template is for. See <a href="/dtt/faqs">Deepthroat Trainer FAQs</a> for related tips.
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Use Template button is disabled">
Check your subscription tier. Importing requires Ultra, Pioneer, or Founder. Upgrade in <a href="https://dashboard.researchanddesire.com/subscription">Subscription</a>.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Your template isn’t getting discovered">
Write a clear description, choose a descriptive title, and set an accurate difficulty so your template surfaces in searches.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
