Once you complete a training session, power off your device and remove the toy if desired. Then visit the <a href="https://dashboard.researchanddesire.com">Research & Desire dashboard</a> to review your performance.

## What you can track

On the dashboard, you can view:

- **Session grades** — see your performance for each session and segment
- **Points earned** — track how many points you added and your total points
- **Leaderboard rankings** — check where you stand across weekly, monthly, and all‑time boards

<Tip>
Invite your partner to log in and review these details too. It's great for accountability and encouragement as you climb the leaderboards.
</Tip>

<Steps>
<Step title="Sign in to your dashboard">
Go to <a href="https://dashboard.researchanddesire.com">dashboard.researchanddesire.com</a> and sign in with your account credentials.

<Check>
You should see your device(s) and recent activity on the dashboard home.
</Check>
</Step>

<Step title="Review your latest training session">
Open Deepthroat Trainer to see your most recent session summary. Select a session to view its grade, points earned, and segment-by-segment performance.

<Info>
If you trained offline, session data won't upload or count toward points until you reconnect. For more on connectivity and data capture, see <a href="/dtt/faqs">Trainer FAQs</a>.
</Info>
</Step>

<Step title="Check your points and rank">
- Learn how points are calculated in <a href="/dashboard/leaderboards/points">Points</a>.
- For competitive tracking, visit <a href="/dashboard/leaderboards/introduction">Leaderboards</a> to see weekly, monthly, and all‑time standings.

<Check>
After a completed, online session, your total points should increase and your leaderboard position may update.
</Check>
</Step>

<Step title="Adjust privacy or notifications (optional)">
- Manage visibility and leaderboard participation in <a href="/dashboard/settings/introduction">Settings</a>.
- Configure email alerts (for rank changes, promotions, and more) in <a href="/dashboard/settings/email">Email Preferences</a>.

<Warning>
If you opt out of public leaderboards, your profile won't appear in public rankings. You'll still see your own results.
</Warning>
</Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="My session didn't appear on the dashboard">
- Confirm your Trainer was online during the session. Offline sessions don't upload data or award points.
- Make sure your device is paired to your account. If needed, follow the <a href="/dtt/quick-start/pairing/pairing-guide">Pairing Guide</a>.
- Refresh the dashboard after a few moments. If it still doesn't appear, contact support from the Help button in the dashboard.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="My points didn't increase">
- Verify the session shows a grade and completion time.
- Review how scoring works in <a href="/dashboard/leaderboards/points">Points</a>. Some modes depend on accuracy, depth, or time in zone.
- Ensure you were connected to Wi‑Fi; reconnect and try another session if needed.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="I don't want to appear on public leaderboards">
Open <a href="/dashboard/settings/introduction">Settings</a> and adjust your privacy preferences to opt out of public leaderboards.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
