## Before you begin

- A clean, smooth, non‑porous surface (glass, acrylic, glazed tile)
- Backboard with suction cups and the trainer unit
- A compatible toy (R+D or your own)
- A charged trainer
- Your dashboard account and 2.4 GHz Wi‑Fi (for syncing results)

<Warning>
Only mount on smooth, non‑porous surfaces. Test suction before use. Stop immediately if you feel pain or discomfort.
</Warning>

## Set up and run your first session

<Steps>
<Step title="Mount the backboard">
Choose a smooth surface at a comfortable height. Lightly moisten the suction cups, press firmly, and check that the board is secure.

<Note>
Suction cups adhere best to clean, non‑textured surfaces. Avoid painted walls or porous materials.
</Note>
</Step>

<Step title="Attach the trainer">
Align the trainer with the backboard and secure it to the hook‑and‑loop (Velcro) mount. Make sure it cannot wobble or shift.
</Step>

<Step title="Install a toy">
Attach a compatible toy to the trainer. If you use your own, set it as your default in the dashboard later so measurements stay accurate. See <a href="/dtt/quick-start/using-toys">Using Different Toys</a>.
</Step>

<Step title="Power on and note your Trainer ID">
Turn the power switch on. During startup, the screen shows your Trainer ID. Write it down—you'll use it to pair.

<Warning>
If the device is plugged in for charging, unplug it before starting a training session. The trainer won't begin training while charging to protect the battery.
</Warning>
</Step>

<Step title="Pair to your dashboard">
Open your dashboard, go to Settings → Devices, and enter the Trainer ID to pair.

<Check>
Done correctly, your device appears under Settings → Devices. If it's already paired to another account, follow the ownership guidance in the FAQs or contact support.
</Check>

<Info>
Step‑by‑step pairing guide: <a href="/dtt/quick-start/pairing/pairing-guide">Pairing Your Trainer</a>
</Info>
</Step>

<Step title="Connect to Wi‑Fi (optional but recommended)">
From the pairing page, follow the Wi‑Fi guide to connect the trainer to a 2.4 GHz network. This enables syncing and updates.

<Info>
Wi‑Fi walkthrough: <a href="/dtt/quick-start/pairing/wifi-setup">Connecting Trainer to Wi‑Fi</a>
</Info>
</Step>

<Step title="Choose a training mode and start">
Pick a simple template (for example, a short Endurance or Repetition segment) and press the start button on the device. Follow on‑screen prompts and indicators during the segment.

<Tip>
Keep early sessions short (1–3 segments). Focus on steady breathing and consistent pacing.
</Tip>
</Step>

<Step title="Review your results">
After the session, open your dashboard to see grades, points, and leaderboards. Use this feedback to adjust difficulty and templates.

<Info>
How to read your results: <a href="/dtt/quick-start/checking-results">Checking Your Results</a>
</Info>
</Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="The suction cups won't stick">
- Clean the surface and cups with mild soap and water; dry fully.
- Lightly moisten cups before mounting and press firmly from the center out.
- Try a different smooth surface (glass, acrylic, glazed tile).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="I don't see my Trainer ID on startup">
- Power the device off and back on; the ID shows during boot.
- Charge the device and try again.
- If still missing, pair using the detailed steps in <a href="/dtt/quick-start/pairing/pairing-guide">Pairing Your Trainer</a>.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Wi‑Fi won't connect">
- Ensure you're using a 2.4 GHz network and correct password.
- Move closer to the router for setup.
- Re‑run the steps in <a href="/dtt/quick-start/pairing/wifi-setup">Connecting Trainer to Wi‑Fi</a>.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Features are missing (Hands‑Free, large templates, etc.)">
Some features require an Ultra‑level subscription. See <a href="https://dashboard.researchanddesire.com/subscription">Subscription</a> and <a href="/dtt/faqs">FAQs</a>.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
