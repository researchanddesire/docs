The Deepthroat Trainer features a 3.5mm accessory port for connecting external devices such as vibrating plugs and wands for positive training reinforcement.

## Official accessories

We currently offer vibrating plugs and wands designed to provide real-time feedback during training sessions. These accessories connect directly to the accessory port and respond automatically based on your performance.

## Custom integrations

If you want to integrate the accessory output into your own project, use the pinout diagram below for reference.

### Port specifications

The accessory port uses a 4-conductor TRRS-style 3.5mm jack with the following characteristics:

| Parameter | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| Connector type | 3.5mm TRRS (4-conductor) |
| Signal output | First ring (R1) |
| Signal type | 3.3V PWM |
| Maximum current | 200mA |
| Behavior | PWM varies based on training performance |

<Warning>
When connecting external devices to the trainer, follow these safety guidelines:

- **Never connect high voltage devices** directly to the trainer
- Follow all applicable electrical safety precautions and local standards
- Only attempt custom integrations if you have experience with electronics and understand the associated risks

We officially support only our R+D accessories. Custom or third-party interfaces are used at your own risk.
</Warning>
