## Troubleshooting

When your Trainer encounters an issue, it displays an error code on screen:

<Card title="E-DTT-1: No Internet" icon="wifi-exclamation" href="../errors/e-dtt-1">
  Your device connected to Wi-Fi but cannot reach the server. Learn how to adjust firewall and router settings.
</Card>

For a complete list of error codes and messages, see the <a href="./error-codes">Error Codes</a> guide.

## Need additional help?

If you've followed the troubleshooting steps and your issue persists, contact our support team with:

- The specific error code displayed on your device
- Your Trainer ID (shown on startup)
- A brief description of the steps you've already tried

<Tip>
Before reaching out, try restarting both your Trainer and router. Many connectivity issues resolve after a fresh reboot.
</Tip>
