Endurance Mode is a timer-based training feature that helps you maintain a comfortable position for a set duration. You’ll practice steady breathing, relaxation, and posture while the app measures your continuity and control.

## How it works

<Steps>
<Step title="Set your target duration">
  Choose a hold time that matches your current comfort level (for example, 10–30 seconds). You can increase the target over time as you improve.

<Check>
  You should see the target duration displayed on the session screen before you begin.
</Check>
</Step>

<Step title="Prepare and position">
  Get into a stable, comfortable position. Relax your shoulders and jaw, and take a few slow breaths through your nose.

<Tip>
  A short relaxation routine (4–5 slow breaths) before each attempt improves consistency.
</Tip>
</Step>

<Step title="Start the session">
  Press Start. Maintain a steady posture while the timer counts down. Short breaks are allowed, but they will reduce your continuity score.

<Warning>
  Stop immediately if you feel pain, dizziness, nausea, or shortness of breath.
</Warning>
</Step>

<Step title="Finish and review">
  When the timer completes, your results are saved to the dashboard for review.

<Check>
  Confirm that the session appears in your History with the correct duration and timestamp.
</Check>
</Step>
</Steps>

## Scoring and results

- Continuity score: Based on how consistently you held the target position without breaks.
- Duration achieved: Longest continuous hold and total time within the session.
- Session grade: Weighted combination of continuity and total duration.

<Info>
Grades are intended to track personal progress over time. Focus on steady improvement rather than single-session results.
</Info>

## Best practices

- Start conservative: Choose a duration you can complete comfortably before increasing.
- Breathe steadily: Inhale through the nose, exhale slowly; avoid breath-holding.
- Use accessories thoughtfully: Optional haptic feedback accessories can provide gentle cues to maintain rhythm and posture.
- Rest between attempts: Take 30–60 seconds between repetitions to reduce fatigue.

## Safety guidelines

<Warning>
Discontinue use and consult a healthcare professional if you experience pain, bleeding, faintness, or persistent discomfort. Do not use while ill or congested. Keep devices clean and follow manufacturer hygiene instructions.
</Warning>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="I can’t reach my target duration">
- Lower the target by 5–10 seconds and build back up gradually.
- Focus on relaxation and nasal breathing before pressing Start.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="My grade drops when I pause">
Short breaks are counted as interruptions. Aim for shorter, more consistent holds and increase duration later.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="The app didn’t save my results">
- Ensure Bluetooth (if applicable) is enabled and the device is connected before starting.
- Check that your battery level is above 20%.
- Retry the session; if the issue persists, sync from the dashboard.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
