Repetition Mode helps you build consistent rhythm through paced insertion and quick withdrawal. You follow an on‑screen target that sets both depth and timing while your trainer measures each rep for accuracy.

<Info>
Repetition Mode is ideal for developing smooth, repeatable motion. If you’re just getting started, see <a href="/dtt/quick-start/introduction">Quick Start</a> first.
</Info>

## Prerequisites

- Your trainer is paired with your dashboard account. See <a href="/dtt/quick-start/pairing/pairing-guide">Pairing your Trainer</a>.
- Your trainer is connected to Wi‑Fi so results upload automatically. See <a href="/dtt/quick-start/pairing/wifi-setup">Connect to Wi‑Fi</a>.
- You’ve selected a toy and completed calibration (if required for your firmware stream).

<Warning>
Calibration is moving to the cloud in the current beta. If you’re on the beta firmware and don’t see local calibration controls, check the status and guidance in <a href="/dtt/software-2.0-preview">Software 2.0.0 Beta Preview</a>.
</Warning>

## How Repetition Mode works

- A moving target on your display guides depth and pace.
- You insert to match the target depth, then withdraw to the start position.
- Each insert/withdraw cycle counts as one repetition.
- Accuracy is calculated from how closely your movement matches the target over time.

<Tip>
Start with slower target speeds and shallow depths. Increase speed and depth in small steps as your accuracy improves.
</Tip>

## Run a repetition session

<Steps>
<Step title="Choose or create a template">
From the dashboard, open a training template configured for Repetition Mode. You can also browse community templates under <a href="/dtt/quick-start/templates">Shareable Templates</a>.
</Step>

<Step title="Set targets (speed, depth, reps)">
Pick a comfortable speed and depth target, then set your repetition count. If your plan calls for progression, increase one variable at a time between sets.
</Step>

<Step title="Prepare and calibrate">
Attach your toy securely. If calibration is available, run it now so the trainer knows the toy tip position and your current comfortable depth.
</Step>

<Step title="Start training and follow the target">
Begin the session. Insert to the indicated depth in sync with the on‑screen cue, then withdraw to the start position. Keep your motion smooth and consistent.

<Check>
You’re on track when the target stays green and your accuracy indicator remains high.
</Check>
</Step>

<Step title="Finish and review your results">
When you complete the set, your grade and points upload to the dashboard. See <a href="/dtt/quick-start/checking-results">Checking Your Results</a> to review trends and leaderboard placement.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Scoring and points

Every repetition contributes to your score. When you maintain high accuracy, your points increase quickly.

- Scoring formula for points: `repetitions × 2 × accuracy`
- Visit <a href="https://dashboard.researchanddesire.com/leaderboards/points">Points</a> for examples and how other modes compare.

<Note>
On‑device you’ll see a grade based on how long you stayed “in the zone.” The dashboard converts performance into points for leaderboards and comparisons.
</Note>

## Best practices

- Breathe steadily and match the target before increasing speed or depth.
- Use shorter sets (8–12 reps) with brief rests to maintain form.
- If your accuracy drops, reduce speed or depth and rebuild consistency.
- Consider enabling Hands‑Free Mode in the template if available to minimize button presses during longer sessions. See <a href="/dtt/faqs">FAQs</a>.

<Warning>
Stop immediately if you feel pain, lightheadedness, or nausea. Resume only when comfortable, and decrease speed/depth targets for the next set.
</Warning>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="The target freezes or slows unexpectedly">
This indicates you’re out of sync. Reduce your speed slightly and focus on matching the target depth before increasing pace.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Calibration option is missing">
You may be on the beta firmware where calibration is handled in the cloud. Check the current status in <a href="/dtt/software-2.0-preview">Software 2.0.0 Beta Preview</a> and proceed with the default calibration if prompted.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Results aren’t appearing on the dashboard">
- Confirm the trainer is connected to Wi‑Fi and shows “online.” See <a href="/dtt/quick-start/pairing/wifi-setup">Connect to Wi‑Fi</a>.
- Power‑cycle the trainer, then run a short 1–2 rep test set.
- Check <a href="/dtt/quick-start/checking-results">Checking Your Results</a> for where to find uploads.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
