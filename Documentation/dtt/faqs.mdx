- Download new training schedules

<Accordion title="What is the segment repeat feature?">
Segment repeat allows you to loop any training segment up to 100 times without manually duplicating it. This keeps your templates clean while enabling longer, more efficient training sessions.

**Example:** Instead of adding 10 identical endurance segments, add one segment and set its repeat value to 10.

| Plan | Maximum Effective Segments |
|------|---------------------------|
| Free | 3 (no repeats) |
| Ultra | 100 (including repeats) |
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What is Pass/Fail Mode?">
Pass/Fail Mode adds accountability by requiring you to meet a minimum performance threshold for each segment.

**How it works:**
- Set a passing grade percentage (0-100%) for each segment
- Meet or exceed the threshold to advance to the next segment
- Fall below the threshold and the segment restarts

**Three-Strike Rule:** Failing the same segment three times consecutively restarts your entire training session from the beginning.

**Custom Failure Messages:** Add personalized feedback (up to 80 characters) that displays on your device when you fail a segment.

<Tip>
Pass/Fail Mode is ideal for building discipline and ensuring consistent performance across all segments.
</Tip>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What is Hands-Free Mode?">
Hands-Free Mode allows your training to progress automatically without pressing buttons on the Trainer. When enabled, segments advance continuously without manual input.

**To use Hands-Free Mode:**
1. Enable it on your training template in the dashboard
2. Allow the 10-second countdown to complete before beginning each segment

<Info>
This feature is exclusive to Ultra subscribers and must be enabled on each template individually.
</Info>
</Accordion>
