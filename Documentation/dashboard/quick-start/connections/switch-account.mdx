A switch account allows you and your partner to control each other's devices bidirectionally. Unlike the standard top/bottom configuration, neither partner is locked into a single role—both can take control at any time.

## Prerequisites

Before setting up a switch account, ensure:

- Both partners have existing accounts
- You already have a standard partner connection established (one partner controls the other)
- Access to the **Settings > Partners** section in your dashboard

## Set up mutual control

<Steps>
<Step title="Navigate to partner settings">
  Log into your account and go to **Settings > Partners**. You'll see your existing partner connection where you can control their devices.
</Step>

<Step title="Create a new invitation">
  Click to create a new invitation to your existing partner. Instead of
  requesting control of their devices, select **Give them access to my
  devices**.
</Step>

<Step title="Send the invitation">
  Click **Send**. Your partner will receive an email with a link to accept the
  invitation.
</Step>

<Step title="Partner accepts the invitation">
  Your partner clicks the email link, which takes them to **Settings > Partners**. They'll see a notification that you want to give them access to your devices. They click **Accept**.

  <Check>
  After accepting, both partners can now control and modify each other's devices.
  </Check>
</Step>

<Step title="Verify mutual control">
  Both partners should now see the account switcher in the top-left corner of the dashboard. Each partner can switch between controlling their own account and their partner's account.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  The account switcher in the top-left corner lets you quickly toggle between
  controlling your devices and your partner's devices.
</Tip>
