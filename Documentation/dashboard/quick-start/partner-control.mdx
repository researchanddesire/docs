## Overview

When a partner grants you control permissions, you can manage their device settings directly from your dashboard. This guide shows you how to switch between accounts and modify settings on your partner's behalf.

## Switching Between Accounts

Once a partner has connected with you and granted control permissions, you'll see a dropdown menu in the top-left corner of the dashboard. Use this menu to switch between managing your own settings and your partner's settings.

<Steps>
<Step title="Verify partner connection">
  Navigate to your account page and confirm your partner is listed under connected users. You should see a message indicating you can control and modify their devices.
</Step>

<Step title="Select the account to manage">
  Use the dropdown menu in the top-left corner to switch between your account
  and your partner's account. On mobile, this option appears in the navigation
  menu.
</Step>

<Step title="Modify settings">
  Once you've selected an account, any changes you make to device settings apply to that account. The page will reload to display the selected user's current configuration.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  When you switch accounts, the page automatically reloads to show the selected
  user's settings. Any changes you save apply only to the currently selected
  account.
</Note>

## Affected Menus

The account selector affects settings on specific pages. When you switch accounts, the following sections display and modify the selected user's settings:

- **Home** — Dashboard overview and status
- **Leaderboards** — Ranking and statistics
- **Chasty Lockbox** — All lockbox menus
- **Deepthroat Trainer** — All trainer menus and templates

<Info>
  All other pages and menus display your personal account settings regardless of
  which account is selected in the dropdown.
</Info>

## Viewing Your Partner's Activity History

When controlling your partner's account, you can view their training activity and session history.

<Steps>
<Step title="Switch to your partner's account">
Use the dropdown menu in the top-left corner to select your partner's account.
</Step>

<Step title="Navigate to the Activity Log">
Go to **Deepthroat Trainer → Activity Log** to view your partner's training history. You'll see session grades, points earned, and segment-by-segment performance.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  If your partner hasn't completed any training sessions yet, the activity log
  will be empty. Activity data only appears after they finish at least one
  online training session.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Activity history is great for accountability and encouragement. Review your
  partner's progress together to celebrate milestones and identify areas for
  improvement.
</Tip>
