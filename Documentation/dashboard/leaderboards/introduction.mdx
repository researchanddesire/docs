Leaderboards transform your activity into competitive rankings. Here's how you can progress from Bronze to Diamond status.

## How it works

### Matches and ranks

- **Matches** are competitive groups of 30 players
- Your goal is to outscore others within your **Rank** tier
- Ranks range from Bronze (entry level) to Diamond (top tier)
- You advance through ranks by earning [points](/dashboard/leaderboards/points)

### Rank progression

Your position at the end of each competition determines whether you move up, stay, or drop down. Here's how many players advance from each rank:

| **Rank**     | **Promotion Zone** | **Demotion Zone** |
| ------------ | ------------------ | ----------------- |
| **Bronze**   | Top 20             | None              |
| **Silver**   | Top 15             | 25th - 30th       |
| **Gold**     | Top 10             | 25th - 30th       |
| **Sapphire** | Top 7              | 25th - 30th       |
| **Ruby**     | Top 7              | 25th - 30th       |
| **Emerald**  | Top 7              | 25th - 30th       |
| **Amethyst** | Top 7              | 25th - 30th       |
| **Pearl**    | Top 7              | 25th - 30th       |
| **Obsidian** | Top 5              | 25th - 30th       |
| **Diamond**  | Top 5              | 25th - 30th       |

<Note>
Bronze is the entry rank—you can't be demoted below it. However, finishing in the bottom positions at any other rank will drop you down one tier.
</Note>

### Competition schedule

- New competitions begin every **Sunday at midnight GMT**
- You join a match automatically when you earn points
- If you're among the first to join, you'll be placed in an open match that fills as others participate

<Tip>
Consistency beats intensity. Regular participation throughout the week often outperforms last-minute point grinding.
</Tip>

## Next steps

Jump in, earn points, and start climbing. Whether you're aiming for Diamond or working your way out of Bronze, every point counts toward your next rank.
