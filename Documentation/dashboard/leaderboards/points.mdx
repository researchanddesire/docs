Training points recognize your effort and progress. You earn them by completing lock and training sessions using your Lockbox and Deepthroat Trainer, as well as through community engagement. Your total determines your position on the global leaderboards.

<Info>
  You can review your current points anytime at Dashboard → Leaderboards → Points
</Info>

## How to earn XP points

You can earn points in three primary ways:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Lockbox sessions" icon="lock">
    Complete any lock session using your Chastity Lockbox. Locks with longer durations generate more points per session. These are unaffected if you take breaks during the session.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Deepthroat training sessions" icon="dumbbell">
    Complete a training session on your Deepthroat Trainer using any training template (and any training segment type) to earn points. Stronger performance yields more points per session.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Community engagement" icon="users">
    Share your Lockbox links publicly and receive votes from the community. Each vote adds to your point total.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Points by Deepthroat Trainer mode

A training session can be composed of one or more training modes (also know as "segments"). Each training mode calculates points from your performance metrics using the formulas below.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Repetition Mode">
    Points reward both your rep count and precision.

    Formula: `repetitions × 2 × accuracy`

    Keep a steady rhythm to maximize the accuracy multiplier.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Bounce Mode">
    Uses the same scoring as Repetition Mode with identical mechanics.

    Formula: `repetitions × 2 × accuracy`

    Find your groove and maintain consistent rhythm throughout the session.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Endurance Mode">
    Rewards sustained effort and training depth over time.

    Formula: `(time in seconds ÷ 2) × depth level`

    Longer sessions at deeper levels yield significantly more points.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Freeform Mode">
    Balances creative expression with rhythmic precision.

    Formula: `(time in seconds ÷ 2) × rhythm accuracy`

    Take your time while keeping consistent timing for the best results.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Leaderboard rankings

Your accumulated points place you across three competitive timeframes:

| Leaderboard | Reset frequency     | Best for                          |
| ----------- | ------------------- | --------------------------------- |
| All‑time    | Never               | Long‑term achievement tracking    |
| Monthly     | First of each month | Medium‑term competition           |
| Weekly      | Every Monday        | Short‑term goals and quick climbs |

<Steps>
  <Step title="Verify your points after a session">

    <Check>
      Open <a href="/dashboard/leaderboards/points">
      Dashboard → Leaderboards → Points</a>

       and confirm your total increased.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Prioritize accuracy over speed. A slower, more precise session typically earns more points than rushing with poor form.
</Tip>

- Train consistently — regular sessions compound your point total over time
- Rotate training modes — variety prevents burnout and builds complementary skills
- Share your progress — community votes provide a passive point stream
- Set incremental goals — celebrate milestones to stay motivated
- Study the leaderboards — observe top performers for inspiration and technique ideas

<Note>
  Every point represents real progress. Focus on consistent improvement, and the leaderboard ranks will follow.
</Note>
