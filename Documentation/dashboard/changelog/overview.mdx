## Lockbox one-click Bluetooth pairing

Pairing a Lockbox is now much simpler. If you're using Chrome or Edge, the dashboard can connect to your Lockbox directly over Bluetooth — no WiFi setup required beforehand.

**How it works:**
- Go to **Settings → Devices** and click **Pair Lockbox**
- On the **Automatic** tab, click **Scan for Lockbox** and select your device from the browser picker
- If your Lockbox is already on WiFi, pairing completes immediately
- If not, enter your WiFi credentials in the dashboard — they're sent securely to the device over Bluetooth

The old manual flow (pairing code) is still available in the **Enter Code** tab for browsers that don't support Bluetooth.

**Also new:**
- **Saved WiFi networks** — store your home network credentials in your account (encrypted) so future pairings are even faster
- Manage saved networks under **Settings → WiFi Networks**
