Manage your account credentials and personal information through the dashboard settings.

## Overview

Your account settings give you control over your core login credentials:

- **Email address** — Update the email associated with your account
- **Password** — Change your password to maintain security

Access both options in the **Private Details** section of your profile settings.

## Change your email address

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to profile settings">
    Click your profile picture in the top-right corner and select **Profile** from the dropdown menu. You'll see the **Private Details** section.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open the email change dialog">
    In the **Email Address** section, click **Change Email**. A dialog box appears showing your current email address.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your new email address">
    Type your new email address in the **New Email Address** field. The system validates the format automatically and requires an address different from your current one.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit the change">
    Click **Update Email** to submit your request. You'll see a loading indicator while the change processes.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify your new email">
    After submitting, you're automatically logged out and redirected to a confirmation page. Check your new email inbox for a verification link and click it to complete the change.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sign back in">
    Once verified, sign in with your new email address. Your previous email address no longer works for login.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
**Email verification required** — You must verify your new email address before you can sign in again. If you don't receive the verification email within a few minutes, check your spam folder.
</Info>

### What to expect

- **Email uniqueness** — Your new email address must not already be in use by another account
- **Automatic logout** — You're logged out immediately after requesting the change for security
- **Verification timing** — The verification link typically arrives within seconds
- **Permanent change** — Once verified, you cannot revert to your old email without going through the change process again

### Troubleshooting email changes

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Didn't receive the verification email?">
    - Check your spam or junk folder
    - Wait a few minutes and check again
    - Confirm you entered the correct email address
    - Contact support if the issue persists
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can't access the new email?">
    If you made a typo in the email address, contact support for assistance. Your old email address won't work for login after initiating the change.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Change your password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to password settings">
    Go to your profile settings by selecting **Profile** then **Private Details**. Click **Change Password** in the Password section to open a dedicated password change page.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your new password">
    Type your new password in the **New Password** field. The system validates your password in real-time. Click the info icon (ℹ️) to view password requirements.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Meet password requirements">
    Your password must include:
    - At least 8 characters
    - One uppercase letter (A-Z)
    - One lowercase letter (a-z)
    - One number (0-9)
    - One special character (!@#$%^&* etc.)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit the change">
    Click **Reset Password** to update your password. After processing, you're redirected to the main dashboard.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Use your new password">
    Your new password takes effect immediately. Use it for all future logins and update any saved passwords in your browser or password manager.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
**Strong password best practices:**
- Use a unique password you don't use elsewhere
- Consider a password manager to generate and store secure passwords
- Avoid personal information like names or birthdays
- Make it memorable but complex
</Tip>

### What to expect

- **Immediate effect** — Password changes take effect instantly
- **No email verification** — Unlike email changes, password updates don't require email verification
- **Old password invalidated** — Your previous password stops working as soon as the new one is set
- **Other devices** — You may need to sign in again on other devices

### Troubleshooting password changes

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Password not meeting requirements?">
    - Verify all requirements are met: length, uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters
    - The system displays specific error messages for each unmet requirement
    - Try a different combination that satisfies all criteria
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can't remember your new password?">
    Use the **Forgot Password** link on the login page. This sends a password reset link to your email, allowing you to set a new password through the reset process.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Security considerations

### Email changes

- **Verification required** — Email changes require verification to prevent unauthorized access
- **Account protection** — Updating your email helps protect against account takeovers
- **Future communications** — All system emails will go to your new address after verification

### Password changes

- **Regular updates** — Consider changing your password periodically for better security
- **Unique passwords** — Never reuse passwords from other accounts
- **Additional security** — Enable two-factor authentication if available

## Getting help

If you encounter issues changing your email or password:

1. Review the troubleshooting sections above for common solutions
2. Verify your internet connection is stable
3. Refresh the page and try again
4. Contact support if problems persist

<Note>
**Need assistance?** Reach out through the support channels in your dashboard or contact the support team directly.
</Note>
