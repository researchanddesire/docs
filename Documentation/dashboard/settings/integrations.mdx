Connect third-party services to your Research & Desire account. Integrations
allow your Chastity Lockbox to stay in sync with external platforms
automatically.

## Chaster Integration

[Chaster](https://chaster.app) is a popular online chastity lock management
platform. When connected, your Chastity Lockbox and Chaster locks stay in sync
automatically.

### What gets synced

| Event                     | Direction     | Description                              |
| ------------------------- | ------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| Lock started on Chaster   | Chaster → R&D | Creates a lock session on your dashboard |
| Lock ended on Chaster     | Chaster → R&D | Ends the lock session on your dashboard  |
| Lock ended on R&D         | R&D → Chaster | Archives the lock on Chaster             |
| Time added/removed (vote) | Both ways     | Keeps the timer in sync                  |
| Freeze / unfreeze         | Chaster → R&D | Keeps remaining time aligned with Chaster |
| Hygiene break started     | Chaster → R&D | Unlocks the lockbox for the break        |
| Hygiene break ended       | Chaster → R&D | Re-locks the lockbox after the break     |

### How to connect

These steps apply once your account has access to the closed beta.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Integrations">
    Go to **Settings → Integrations** in your dashboard.
  </Step>

<Step title="Click Connect Chaster">
  Click the **Connect Chaster** button on the Chaster card.
</Step>

<Step title="Authorize on Chaster">
  You will be redirected to Chaster to authorize the connection. Sign in and
  approve the request.
</Step>

  <Step title="Start a lock on Chaster">
    Create a lock on Chaster and add the **Research And Desire Lockbox**
    extension. Your lockbox session will start automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### How to disconnect

You can disconnect your Chaster account at any time from the Integrations page.
Two options are available:

- **Disconnect** — Removes the link but preserves your historical lock data.
- **Disconnect & purge data** — Permanently deletes all Chaster-related data
  from Research & Desire. This cannot be undone.

<Note>
  You cannot disconnect while a Chaster-controlled lock session is active. End
  or abandon the session first.
</Note>

### Requirements

- A paired **Chastity Lockbox** on your R&D account
- A **Chaster** account at [chaster.app](https://chaster.app)

### Known limitations

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Locks must be started on Chaster">
    To get full bidirectional sync, start your lock on Chaster with the
    Research And Desire Lockbox extension. Locks started on the R&D dashboard
    will not appear on Chaster.
  </Accordion>

<Accordion title="Hygiene breaks must be initiated from Chaster">
  Break start/end events are controlled by Chaster. You cannot start a hygiene
  break from the R&D dashboard for a Chaster-controlled session.
</Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does freeze on Chaster affect my Lockbox timer?">
    Yes. **Freeze** and **unfreeze** on Chaster are separate from add/remove time
    votes, but they update Chaster's remaining lock duration. Those updates
    sync to Research & Desire, so your dashboard and Lockbox **stay aligned with
    Chaster's remaining time** while frozen or after you unfreeze.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Some Chaster extensions are not synced">
    Only time changes, hygiene breaks, and voting events are synced. Other
    Chaster extensions (games, tasks, etc.) operate independently on Chaster.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I can't find the Research And Desire Lockbox extension on Chaster">
    The extension is part of the same closed beta as dashboard linking. If it
    does not appear when you create or edit a lock on Chaster, your Chaster
    account is likely not in the current test group. Watch
    [Discord](https://discord.gg/researchanddesire) for rollout news, or email
    support@researchanddesire.com if you need help.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Chaster isn't syncing to my Lockbox">
    Sync requires an active Chaster-controlled session started with the Research
    And Desire Lockbox extension, and a working link under **Settings →
    Integrations**. If you don't have beta access, the extension and reliable
    sync will not be available yet.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
