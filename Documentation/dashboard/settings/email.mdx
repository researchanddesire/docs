## Overview

You can manage your email notification preferences directly from the [Research and Desire dashboard](https://dashboard.researchanddesire.com). Enable or disable all notifications globally, or customize which specific categories you want to receive.

## Access email settings

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Settings">
    Go to [dashboard.researchanddesire.com](https://dashboard.researchanddesire.com) and select **Settings** from the navigation menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Advanced settings">
    Select **Advanced** within the Settings section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Locate Email Settings">
    Find the **Email Settings** section in the middle of the page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Email controls

### Global toggle

Use the main toggle to enable or disable **all** email notifications from Research and Desire with a single click. When disabled, you won't receive any emails regardless of individual category settings.

### Test email

Select **Send Test Email** to verify your email configuration is working correctly. You should receive a confirmation email in your inbox within a few minutes.

<Tip>
If you don't receive the test email, check your spam folder and verify your email address is correct in your account settings.
</Tip>

## Notification categories

Customize exactly which types of emails you receive by toggling individual categories on or off.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Leaderboard events">
    Receive notifications about significant changes to your leaderboard position:
    - Entering the promotion or demotion zone
    - Major rank changes
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Lockbox events">
    Stay informed about lockbox activity for you and your partners:
    - Lock and unlock events
    - Schedule changes
    - Partner activity updates
    
    <Info>
    Each user must enable this setting in their **own** account to receive notifications. If you're a Keyholder who wants to receive notifications about your partner's lockbox, enable this option in your own dashboard settings. The lockbox owner enabling this setting will not send notifications to their partners—each user controls their own notifications independently.
    </Info>
    
    <Note>
    Lockbox email notifications are configured exclusively through the dashboard. There are no notification settings on the lockbox device itself.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Trainer events">
    Get notified about training sessions:
    - Training session starts (for you or your partners)
    - Training mode changes
    - Device connection status
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Sharing events">
    Receive notifications when someone invites you to connect:
    - Play invitations
    - Partner connection requests
    
    <Note>
    This category only applies to invitations you receive, not invitations you send.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Marketing emails">
    Subscribe to promotional communications:
    - Promotional codes and discounts
    - New product announcements
    - Company updates
    
    <Info>
    This subscribes you to our third-party marketing email list. You can unsubscribe at any time.
    </Info>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

If you're not receiving expected notifications, work through these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Verify the global toggle">
    Confirm the main email toggle is enabled. If disabled, no emails will be sent regardless of category settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check category settings">
    Ensure the specific notification categories you want are enabled.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send a test email">
    Use the test email feature to confirm your email configuration is working.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your spam folder">
    Emails from Research and Desire may be filtered by your email provider. Mark them as "not spam" to improve delivery.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your email address">
    Confirm your account has the correct email address on file.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
If you've tried all these steps and still aren't receiving emails, contact support for assistance.
</Warning>
