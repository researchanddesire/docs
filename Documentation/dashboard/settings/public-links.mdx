---
title: "Public Links Opt-Out"
description: "Control whether your lock appears in the public voting directory or remains accessible only via direct link"
---

When you enable **Public Link**, your lock appears in the public voting directory by default. This makes it discoverable by anyone browsing the directory. If you prefer more control over who can find your lock, you can opt out of the directory while still sharing via direct link.

## Directory visibility options

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Public directory (default)">
    Your lock is fully discoverable:
    - Appears in the public voting directory
    - Direct link sharing works
    - Anyone can discover and vote on your lock
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Private link only">
    Your lock remains hidden from discovery:
    - Only accessible via direct link
    - Does not appear in the public directory
    - Share with specific people you choose
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Configure your visibility settings

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open lock settings">
    Navigate to **Chastity Lockbox** → **Settings** in your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable public link">
    Turn on the **Public Link** toggle to activate link sharing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access visibility options">
    Click the **Settings icon** (⚙️) next to the Public Link toggle.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Opt out of directory listing">
    Toggle **Show in Public Directory** off to hide your lock from the public directory.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
Your visibility setting is applied when you start the lock session. Changes made after starting won't affect the current session.
</Info>

## Important considerations

- **Direct links always work** — Regardless of your directory visibility setting, anyone with your direct link can access and vote on your lock.
- **Functionality remains unchanged** — This setting only affects discoverability, not how the lock itself operates.
- **Privacy through obscurity** — Opting out means people can't stumble upon your lock, but it's not password-protected.
