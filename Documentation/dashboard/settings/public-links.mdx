---
title: "Public Links Opt-Out"
description: "Control whether your lock appears in the public voting directory or remains accessible only via direct link"
---

Choose how discoverable your lock is when you share it. Enabling Public Link generates a shareable URL for your lock. By default, locks with a public link appear in the public voting directory so others can find and vote. You can opt out of the directory while keeping the direct link active.

<Note>
  Terminology: “Public Link” is the shareable URL for your lock; the “public
  voting directory” is the browseable list where eligible public locks appear.
</Note>

## Directory visibility options

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Public directory (default)">
    Your lock is fully discoverable: - Appears in the public voting directory -
    Direct link sharing works - Anyone browsing the directory can view and vote
    on your lock
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Private link only">
    Your lock remains hidden from discovery: - Not listed in the public
    directory - Only accessible via its direct link - Share privately with
    specific people you choose
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Configure your visibility settings

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open lock settings">
    In the dashboard, go to **Chastity Lockbox → Templates** (or **Chastity Lockbox → Home → Edit** for the active lock’s template).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable Public Link">
    Turn on the **Public Link** (or **Enable public voting on lock duration**) toggle to activate link sharing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open visibility options">
    Click the **settings icon** (⚙️) next to the Public Link toggle to configure visibility.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Opt out of directory listing">
    Toggle **Show in Public Directory** off to hide your lock from the public directory.

    <Check>
    Verification: Copy your lock’s public link and open it in a private/incognito window to confirm it loads. Then browse the public directory to confirm your lock does not appear.
    </Check>

  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Your visibility choice is applied when you start the lock session. Changes
  made after starting won’t affect the current session; create a new session to
  apply updated visibility.
</Info>

## Important considerations

- **Direct links always work** — Anyone with your link can access and vote, even if the lock is hidden from the directory.
- **Functionality remains unchanged** — This setting only affects discoverability; the lock’s behavior and voting rules stay the same.
- **Privacy through obscurity** — Opting out prevents casual discovery but does not add authentication or a password.

<Warning>
  Treat direct links as shareable. Recipients can forward your link to others.
  If you need stricter controls, share carefully and review your settings before
  starting a session.
</Warning>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I can’t find the Public Link or visibility option">
  - Make sure you’re editing the lock’s template in **Chastity Lockbox → Templates**.
  - If a session is already active, some settings may be read-only until you create a new session.
  - Confirm you have permission to edit the lock (owner or keyholder).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My changes didn’t apply to the current session">
  Visibility is set at session start. End the current session (or wait for it to complete), then start a new session with the updated visibility.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
