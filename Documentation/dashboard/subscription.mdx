## Our commitment to you

Your device, the dashboard, and all core features are free—and always will be. We believe great technology should be accessible to everyone, so we never gate core functionality behind a paywall.

Subscriptions unlock extras and customizations while supporting ongoing development. If you subscribe, you get perks like advanced training options, early access to features, and a voice in shaping what comes next. If you don’t, that’s perfectly fine—we’re glad you’re here.

<Info>
Core training, device pairing, and day‑to‑day use remain free for all users. Subscriptions add conveniences and power‑user capabilities.
</Info>

### Ultra

The Ultra tier delivers powerful customization options and advanced training features:

- Avatar flair — display your Ultra status with exclusive profile flair
- Beta access — try new features before general release
- Custom consent policies — configure the platform to match your preferences
- AI training schedule control — set when and how AI training occurs
- Flexible data export — export your data in the format you need
- New training modes — access experimental training experiences
- Deepthroat Trainer — create up to 100 segments (standard: 3)
- Extended repetitions — repeat segments up to 100 times for longer, more efficient training templates
- Hands‑Free Mode — enable continuous training sessions without manual input
- Pass/Fail options — add pass/fail criteria to individual segments
- Chastity Lockbox — custom lock animation colors to personalize your device display

<Note>
Ultra (and select staff/founder tiers) can import public community templates from Explore. See <a href="/deepthroat-trainer/training-modes/shareable-templates">Shareable Templates</a>.
</Note>

### Pioneer

The Pioneer tier is designed for enthusiasts who want to influence product direction:

- Pioneer avatar flair — stand out with exclusive profile recognition
- Alpha access — test features before they reach beta
- Feature voting — vote on upcoming development priorities
- Community membership — join our innovation community
- All Ultra benefits — includes everything from the Ultra tier

<Accordion title="How to access Pioneer benefits">
  **Join the R+D Discord** — Pioneer members receive an invite to our private R+D Discord server where development discussions happen. Check your email for the invite link after subscribing, or visit **Settings → Subscription** in the dashboard to access it.

  <DiscordInvite community="rad" />

  **Alpha features** — When new features enter alpha testing, they appear in your dashboard with an "Alpha" badge. You'll also receive announcements in the R+D Discord when new alpha features are available.

  **Feature voting** — Participate in feature prioritization through polls and discussions in the R+D Discord. Your input directly influences what we build next.

  **Stay informed** — The R+D Discord includes channels for development updates, roadmap discussions, and early previews of upcoming features.
</Accordion>

### Founder

The Founder tier places you at the center of our development process:

- Founder avatar flair — display the most prestigious profile flair
- Experimental features — access features directly from our lab
- R&D office hours — chat with our research and development team twice monthly
- Platform guidance — help shape the future direction of the product
- All Pioneer and Ultra benefits — includes everything from both tiers

## Subscriptions and partner connections

When you grant a partner control over your devices, they can use your subscription features while managing your account.

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Can my partner use my Ultra features?">
Yes. When your partner switches to your account view to control your devices, they have access to any subscription features your account has. Your partner acts through your account, so they can use Ultra features like custom lock animation colors or advanced training options as long as your account is subscribed.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Does my partner need their own subscription?">
No, your partner does not need their own subscription to control your devices and use your features. However, if they want Ultra features on their own account (for their own devices or self-locks), they need their own subscription.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What about features on my partner's own account?">
Subscription benefits only apply to the subscribed account. If your partner is not subscribed, they won't have access to Ultra features when viewing their own account—only when controlling yours.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Info>
For details on setting up partner connections, see <a href="/dashboard/quick-start/connections/managing-partners">Managing Partner Connections</a>.
</Info>

## Subscribe in a few steps

<Steps>
<Step title="Open Subscription settings">
Sign in to the dashboard, then go to <strong>Settings → Subscription</strong>.

<Check>
You’re in the right place if you see available tiers (Ultra, Pioneer, Founder).
</Check>
</Step>

<Step title="Choose your tier">
Select the plan that matches your goals. You can review included benefits before confirming.
</Step>

<Step title="Confirm and activate">
Complete checkout to activate your subscription. Accepted payment methods include credit card, Google Pay, and Link.

<Check>
After activation: you’ll see your avatar flair, Ultra‑only toggles (like Hands‑Free Mode), and options such as AI schedule control on relevant pages.
</Check>
</Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Choose annual billing to receive 2 months free.
</Tip>

<Note>
PayPal is available for purchases in the R+D Store but is not currently supported for subscription payments.
</Note>

<Warning>
Some features—like importing community templates or segment repeat—require an active Ultra (or higher) subscription and must be enabled per template in the dashboard.
</Warning>
