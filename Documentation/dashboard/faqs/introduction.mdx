## Account Management

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="How do I create a dashboard account?">
To create a dashboard account:

1. Visit the [dashboard website](https://dashboard.researchanddesire.com/app)
2. Click **Create a free account**
3. Enter your email address and choose a password
4. Click **Create account**
5. Check your email for a confirmation link
6. Click the link to verify your account

<Note>
If you encounter any issues creating an account, contact our support team at [support@researchanddesire.com](mailto:support@researchanddesire.com).
</Note>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I reset my password?">
To reset your password while logged in:

1. Navigate to **Settings > My Profile**
2. Scroll to the bottom of the page
3. Click **Change Password**
4. Enter your new password
5. Click **Reset Password** to confirm
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="I forgot my password. How do I recover my account?">
You can recover your account using a magic link:

1. Go to the [dashboard sign-in page](https://dashboard.researchanddesire.com/app)
2. Enter your email address
3. Click **Email link**
4. Check your email for a message containing a magic link and sign-in code
5. Sign in using either the link or the code

Once signed in, you can change your password in **Settings > My Profile**.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I change my username?">
To update your username:

1. Navigate to **Settings > My Profile**
2. Click on your current username (the text field is editable)
3. Type your new username
4. Save your changes
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do I need to use the same email for my dashboard and R+D purchases?">
No, you can use different email addresses for your dashboard account and your Research + Desire purchases. The accounts are not required to match.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I delete my account?">
Account deletion requires assistance from our support team. To request account deletion:

1. Contact support at [support@researchanddesire.com](mailto:support@researchanddesire.com)
2. Include the email address of the account you want deleted
3. Our team will process the request and confirm once complete

<Warning>
Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All data associated with the account will be removed.
</Warning>

<Tip>
If you created a duplicate account by mistake and want to use that email address with a different account, delete the duplicate first, then update your email address in **Settings > Profile > Private Details**.
</Tip>
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Accordion title="Is there a mobile app for the dashboard?">
A dedicated mobile app is not currently available—the dashboard is a web-based platform accessed through any modern browser. No download or app store is required.

Visit [dashboard.researchanddesire.com](https://dashboard.researchanddesire.com) from any device with an internet connection to access your account.

<Note>
The dashboard works internationally and is accessible from most countries worldwide. Both you and your partner can use the dashboard from different locations.
</Note>

Mobile app development is on our roadmap for future releases.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Why does my scheduled template show the wrong day?">
The dashboard uses GMT (Greenwich Mean Time) for all scheduling features. If you start a session in the evening in your local timezone, the dashboard may already consider it the next day in GMT.

For example, if you're in a timezone that is GMT-5 and start a session at 10 PM Wednesday local time, the dashboard sees it as 3 AM Thursday GMT and loads Thursday's scheduled template instead.

<Note>
Per-user timezone settings are not currently available. Plan scheduled templates with GMT timing in mind, or use manual template selection if you train late in the evening.
</Note>
