# Reset your password

If you've forgotten your dashboard password, you have two options: log in with a magic link or reset your password entirely.

## Log in with a magic link

You can bypass password entry by using the email link option on the login screen.

<Steps>
<Step title="Select Email link">
  On the login page, click **Email link** instead of entering your password.

  <Frame>
    <img src="./_images/Screenshot 2025-09-10 084944.jpg" alt="Login screen showing the Email link option" width="283" />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Check your email">
  You'll receive an email with a secure link. Click the link to log in without entering your password.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Magic links expire after a short time. If yours has expired, request a new one from the login page.
</Tip>

## Reset your password

If you prefer to set a new password, you can do so from your profile settings.

<Steps>
<Step title="Navigate to your profile">
  Go to **Settings > My Profile** in your dashboard.

  <Frame>
    <img src="./_images/Screenshot 2025-09-10 085132.jpg" alt="My Profile page showing the Change Password button" />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Change your password">
  Scroll to the bottom of the page and click **Change Password**.
</Step>

<Step title="Confirm your new password">
  Enter your new password, then click **Reset Password** to save your changes.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Check>
Your password has been updated. Use your new password the next time you log in.
</Check>
