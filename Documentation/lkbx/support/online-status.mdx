Knowing whether your Chastity Lockbox is online ensures you can send settings and receive updates reliably. This guide shows two quick ways to confirm status: from the dashboard and directly on the device.

## Check the dashboard indicator

Use the dashboard to confirm your device is connected to your account and currently online.

<Steps>
<Step title="Confirm the device is paired">
Sign in to the dashboard and go to <strong>Settings → Devices</strong>. Verify your Lockbox appears as paired.

<Check>
You should see your Lockbox listed with its ID.
</Check>
</Step>

<Step title="Open the Chastity Lockbox home page">
Navigate to the Lockbox home in the dashboard.
</Step>

<Step title="Find the online/offline indicator">
Look for the connection indicator in the bottom-left corner of the page. When your device is online, the dashboard will show "Online"; when not connected, it will show "Offline".

<Note>
The dashboard status may take up to 60 seconds to update after your device connects. No page refresh is required—it syncs automatically.
</Note>
</Step>
</Steps>

## Check the device display (real-time)

Your device display shows real-time connectivity and is the most accurate source of truth.

<Steps>
<Step title="Wake the device">
Power on the Lockbox or wake it from sleep.
</Step>

<Step title="Wait for Wi‑Fi">
When Wi‑Fi connects, the Wi‑Fi icon appears.
</Step>

<Step title="Read the status indicator">
Look for the small round status indicator next to the Wi‑Fi icon (usually to its left) on the device display.

<Check>
A solid green dot next to the Wi‑Fi icon means the device is online and connected.
</Check>
</Step>
</Steps>

### Status indicator meanings

- Flashing yellow dot: connecting to the dashboard
- Solid green dot: online and connected
- No dot visible: offline, or the device is asleep (wake it to check again)

<Tip>
Trust the device display over the dashboard indicator. The device shows live status; the dashboard can lag by up to a minute.
</Tip>

<Info>
About sleep modes: in <a href="/chastity-lockbox/technical-documentation/device-states/sleep-mode">Automatic Sleep</a> the device may dim the screen but typically stays connected; in <strong>Deep Sleep</strong> Wi‑Fi is turned off until you wake the device.
</Info>

## Troubleshooting

If your device shows offline status or the dashboard says "Offline":

- Make sure the device is awake and the Wi‑Fi icon appears
- Move the device closer to your router or try a different network
- Reconnect Wi‑Fi from the device menu (see Quick Start Step 1)
- Wait up to 60 seconds for the dashboard to sync
- Restart the device if needed

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Reconnect Wi‑Fi">
Go to the device menu and run Wi‑Fi setup again. If you still can’t connect, see <a href="/all-devices/troubleshooting/wi-fi-trouble-shooting">Wi‑Fi Trouble Shooting</a>.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="The Wi‑Fi icon never appears">
- Verify your network credentials
- Try a 2.4 GHz network if available
- Power cycle your router
- Restart the Lockbox (see <a href="/chastity-lockbox/technical-documentation/device-states/restarting-device">Restarting Device</a>)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="The device might be asleep">
If you enabled Deep Sleep, Wi‑Fi is off until you wake the device. See <a href="/chastity-lockbox/technical-documentation/device-states/sleep-mode">Sleep Mode</a>.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Still offline after restart">
If the device remains offline after a restart and Wi‑Fi reconnection, check for Wi‑Fi related errors (e.g., E‑Lock 10) under <a href="/chastity-lockbox/technical-documentation/device-errors/e-lock-10-could-not-connect-to-wi-fi">Device Errors</a>, or contact support.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
