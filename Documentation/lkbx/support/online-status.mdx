## Check the dashboard indicator

The quickest way to verify your device's status is through the dashboard:

1. Log into your account and navigate to **Settings > My Devices** to confirm your lockbox is paired
2. Go to the **Chastity Lockbox home page**
3. Look for the **online/offline indicator** in the bottom left corner

<Note>
The dashboard indicator may take up to 60 seconds to update. No page refresh is required—it syncs automatically.
</Note>

## Check the device display

Your lockbox display provides real-time connection feedback:

1. **Wake the device** from sleep mode by powering it on
2. **Wait for Wi-Fi** to connect (the Wi-Fi icon will appear)
3. **Watch the status indicator** below the Wi-Fi icon in the bottom left corner

### Status indicator meanings

| Indicator | Status |
|-----------|--------|
| Yellow flashing dot | Connecting to dashboard |
| Solid green dot | Online and connected |
| No indicator | Offline or in sleep mode |

<Tip>
Always trust the status indicator on your device display over the dashboard indicator. The device shows real-time status, while the dashboard may have a slight delay.
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting

If your device shows offline status:

- Verify your Wi-Fi network is working
- Ensure the device is awake and not in sleep mode
- Check that the Wi-Fi icon appears on the device display
- Wait up to 60 seconds for the dashboard to sync
