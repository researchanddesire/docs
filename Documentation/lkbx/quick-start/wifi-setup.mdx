## What You'll Need

- A fully charged Lockbox
- A smartphone or laptop
- Access to a **2.4 GHz Wi-Fi network** (5 GHz networks are not supported)

<Warning>
The Lockbox only supports 2.4 GHz Wi-Fi networks. If your router broadcasts both 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz bands, make sure you connect to the 2.4 GHz network.
</Warning>

## Setup Instructions

<Steps>
<Step title="Open Wi-Fi settings on your Lockbox">
  Power on your Lockbox and navigate to **Settings** → **Wi-Fi Settings**.

  The Lockbox will create a temporary network called **Lockbox Setup** and display a QR code on screen.
</Step>

<Step title="Connect to the Lockbox network">
  On your phone or laptop, either:
  - Scan the QR code displayed on the Lockbox, or
  - Manually connect to the **Lockbox Setup** network in your device's Wi-Fi settings
</Step>

<Step title="Configure your Wi-Fi credentials">
  A configuration portal will automatically open—similar to connecting to Wi-Fi at a coffee shop or library.

  1. Select **Configure Wi-Fi**
  2. Choose your 2.4 GHz home network from the list
  3. Enter your Wi-Fi password
  4. Tap **Save**

  The Lockbox will receive your credentials and attempt to connect.
</Step>

<Step title="Verify the connection">
  Once connected, the Lockbox will display a Wi-Fi icon with full signal bars.

  <Check>
  If you see full Wi-Fi bars with no line through them, your Lockbox is successfully connected to the internet.
  </Check>

  <Note>
  You may notice a small dot beneath the Wi-Fi icon. This indicates sync status and will be covered in a future guide.
  </Note>
</Step>
</Steps>
