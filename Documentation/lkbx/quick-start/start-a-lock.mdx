---
title: "How to Start a Lock from the Dashboard"
description: "Learn how to start a lock using lock templates on your own account"
---

This guide walks you through starting a lock using templates you've created in your Chastity Lockbox dashboard.

## Prerequisites

Before starting a lock, ensure:

- You have a lockbox paired to your account (visible in **Settings \> Devices**)
- Your lockbox is online and connected to Wi-Fi
- You have at least one lock template created

<Tip>
  You can verify your device is online by checking the indicator in the top right corner of the lockbox display. A **green, stable dot** means you're ready to receive settings. If the dot is **yellow and blinking**, your device is not ready—check the [WiFi setup guide](/lkbx/quick-start/wifi-setup) for help.
</Tip>

## Instantly start a 24-hour lock

<Step title="Navigate to the Chastity Lockbox homepage">
  From the dashboard home page, under the "Chastity Lockbox" section, click on "**Home**".
</Step>

<Step title='Click the "Start 24-hour lock session'>
  If you have a paired lockbox to this account, you will see a black "Start 24-hour lock session" button. Clicking this will immediately lock your device for 24 hours!

  <Warning>
    Warning: Unless it is your partner who clicks this while browsing your dashboard, you will be unable to cancel or modify this 24-hour lock.
  </Warning>
</Step>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Templates">
    From the dashboard home page, under the "Chastity Lockbox" section, click on "**Templates**", where you'll see your saved lock configurations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your template">
    Find the template you want to use for this lock session (or create a custom one). Each template contains your predefined lock settings.

    <Note>
      Need to create a template first? Templates can be created in the dashboard under **Chastity Lockbox \> Templates**.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your lock mode">
    Click the **Lock** button on the right side of your chosen template. You'll have two options:

    - **No keyholder** — Start a self-lock where you maintain control
    - **Select a keyholder** — Choose from your available partners to hold the key

    <Info>
      Self-locking is a great option for testing your settings without consequences.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm and start">
    Click **Confirm** to start the lock. Your device will automatically receive the settings.

    <Check>
      Your lock is now active! Test the device to verify it's locked before proceeding with your session.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>
