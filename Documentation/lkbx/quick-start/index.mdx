Welcome to Chastity Lockbox! The Lockbox is a smart keyholding device designed to simplify self-keyholding and enable remote play with a partner—from anywhere in the world.

## Setting Up Wi-Fi

The Lockbox is most powerful when connected to your online dashboard at [dashboard.researchanddesire.com](https://dashboard.researchanddesire.com).

<Steps>
<Step title="Access the Wi-Fi setup menu">
  Navigate to the Wi-Fi setup option on your Lockbox display.
</Step>

<Step title="Connect your phone to the device">
  Use your phone to connect to the Lockbox's temporary network.
</Step>

<Step title="Configure your network">
  Enter your Wi-Fi credentials to connect the Lockbox to your home network.

  <Warning>
  The Lockbox requires a **2.4GHz Wi-Fi connection**. 5GHz networks are not supported.
  </Warning>
</Step>
</Steps>

## Lock Modes

### Lock (Offline Capable)

You can lock the box at any time—with or without a Wi-Fi connection—by selecting **Lock** from the menu. Set the duration of your lock session, and the timer begins immediately.

### Lock Forever (Online Only)

Lock Forever secures the device and defers all unlock decisions to your preconfigured online settings. Only your keyholder or dashboard rules can release the lock.

<Warning>
Be careful with this mode—you could end up stuck in chastity until your keyholder decides otherwise.
</Warning>

### Sleep Mode

Sleep mode puts your device into a low-power state, ideal for battery-only use. In sleep mode, the battery can last several days while continuing to track your lock timer without an internet connection.

## Important Reminders

<Note>
**Practice safe chastity:**
- Know how your body responds and practice good hygiene to stay locked longer safely.
- Always have a backup release method in case you need urgent access to your key.
- The Lockbox has an emergency access port on the bottom that you can break open with a screwdriver in case of device failure or power loss. Replacement covers are available for purchase.
</Note>

## Unlocking Your Device

The device unlocks only when:
- The timer expires, or
- Your keyholder unlocks it through the dashboard

<Tip>
Keyholders can unlock the device at any time, regardless of the lock timer status.
</Tip>

### Emergency Unlocking

#### Software Emergency Unlock

In an emergency, you can unlock the Lockbox by holding the lock button for 2 seconds and selecting **Emergency Unlock**.

<Warning>
Using the emergency unlock triggers a **24-hour cooldown period** during which the device cannot be used.
</Warning>

#### Hardware Emergency Unlock

If the device fails or loses power, you can physically unlock it using the access port on the back of the device. Insert a screwdriver into the hole and break off the bottom piece to release the lock mechanism.

## Troubleshooting

### Wi-Fi Connection Issues

If you're having trouble connecting:

- **Move closer to your router** — Signal strength matters during initial setup.
- **Verify your network is 2.4GHz** — The Lockbox does not support 5GHz networks.
- **Test your password** — Try connecting another device to confirm your credentials are correct.
