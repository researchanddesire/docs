Once your partner has paired their Lockbox to their account, they can grant you control over their device. Follow these steps to become their keyholder.

## Connect to your partner's account

<Steps>
<Step title="Create your keyholder account">
  Sign up for your own account and log in. You need a separate account from your partner to act as their keyholder.
</Step>

<Step title="Send a partner invitation">
  1. Navigate to **Settings** > **Partners** from your dashboard menu
  2. Click **Create an invitation**
  3. Enter your partner's email address or username
  4. Under **Access Type**, select **Request access to their devices**
  5. Click **Send invitation**
</Step>

<Step title="Partner accepts the invitation">
  Your partner must sign into their account (where the Lockbox is paired) and accept your invitation.

  <Check>
  Once accepted, your username appears on their **Settings** > **Partners** page with the message "This user can control and modify your devices."
  </Check>
</Step>
</Steps>

## Control your partner's Lockbox

After your partner accepts the invitation, you can manage their devices from your own account.

<Steps>
<Step title="Switch to your partner's account view">
  Click the **Your Account** dropdown in the top-left navigation bar, then select your partner's account from the list.
</Step>

<Step title="Manage lock sessions">
  From your partner's account view, you can:
  
  - View their current session and stats on the **Chastity Lockbox** > **Home** page
  - Create new lock sessions using the **Chastity Lockbox** > **Templates** page
  
  See [How to start a lock from the dashboard](./start-a-lock) for detailed instructions.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Return control after Give Up Control (Permanent consent)

If your partner enabled **Give Up Control** with **Permanent** consent, only you can return full control to them from the dashboard—they cannot toggle it off themselves.

1. Use the top-left account menu to switch to **your partner's account** (the lockee account that owns the Lockbox)
2. Open **Settings** > **Advanced**
3. Turn off **Give Up Control**

You must perform these steps while viewing their account, not only from your own. If the option is unavailable or does not work, ask your partner to contact [support@researchanddesire.com](mailto:support@researchanddesire.com) from their dashboard email; support can reset the setting when needed.

See also [Give Up Control](/lkbx/faqs#what-happens-if-i-use-the-give-up-control-feature) in the Lockbox FAQs.
