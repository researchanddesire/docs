The header bar on your lockbox displays several icons that indicate the current state of your device. Understanding these symbols helps you quickly assess whether your lockbox is locked, connected, and ready for use.

From left to right, the icons are:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Lock icon">
    Indicates whether your device is currently locked. The status indicator shows whether the backplate is properly positioned. See [Lock icon](#lock-icon) for details.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Wi-Fi icon">
    Shows whether your device is connected to a Wi-Fi network. The status indicator reveals whether you're also connected to the dashboard and ready to play. See [Wi-Fi icon](#wi-fi-icon) for details.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Battery icon">
    Displays the current battery level and charging status. This icon has no status indicator. See [Battery icon](#battery-icon) for details.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Lock icon

The lock icon shows the current status of your device's sensors. This icon reflects the physical state of the lock mechanism—not your active play session.

### Locked

<Frame>
  <img src="../../.gitbook/assets/locked.png" alt="Locked icon showing the device is secured" />
</Frame>

When you see this symbol, the locking mechanism is engaged. Your device's sensors have detected that the lock is secured and the contents cannot be accessed.

### Unlocked

<Frame>
  <img src="../../.gitbook/assets/unlocked.png" alt="Unlocked icon showing the device can be opened" />
</Frame>

The unlocked symbol indicates that the sensors have detected the lock is open. You can access the contents of the box.

### Backplate on

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Frame>
    <img src="../../.gitbook/assets/locked backplate on.png" alt="Locked state with backplate detected" />
  </Frame>
  <Frame>
    <img src="../../.gitbook/assets/unlocked backplate on.png" alt="Unlocked state with backplate detected" />
  </Frame>
</CardGroup>

A small status indicator appears in the bottom-left corner of the lock icon. When this indicator is **green**, the sensors have detected that the backplate is properly positioned and the device is ready to lock. This indicator can appear in both locked and unlocked states.

### Backplate off

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Frame>
    <img src="../../.gitbook/assets/locked backplate off (1).png" alt="Locked state with backplate removed" />
  </Frame>
  <Frame>
    <img src="../../.gitbook/assets/unlocked backplate off.png" alt="Unlocked state with backplate removed" />
  </Frame>
</CardGroup>

When the status indicator is **solid yellow**, the sensors have detected that the backplate is not in position. Attempting to lock in this state will fail.

## Wi-Fi icon

### Wi-Fi not connected

<Frame>
  <img src="../../.gitbook/assets/wifi-off (1).png" alt="Wi-Fi disconnected icon" />
</Frame>

This symbol indicates your device is not connected to any Wi-Fi network. This typically occurs when:

- Your lockbox is brand new and has never been configured for Wi-Fi
- Your lockbox was previously connected but is now out of range of the saved network

In either case, you'll need to configure Wi-Fi by following the [Wi-Fi setup guide](/lkbx/quick-start/wifi-setup).

### Wi-Fi connected

<Frame>
  <img src="../../.gitbook/assets/wifi (1).png" alt="Wi-Fi connected icon" />
</Frame>

This symbol confirms your device is connected to Wi-Fi and can access the internet.

<Note>
Wi-Fi connectivity alone doesn't mean your device can receive commands. You also need to pair your device and connect it to the dashboard. Follow the [pairing guide](/lkbx/quick-start/pairing) for instructions.
</Note>

Check the status indicator in the bottom-left corner of the Wi-Fi icon to see if your device is ready to receive commands.

#### Status indicator: flashing yellow

<Frame>
  <img src="../../.gitbook/assets/wifi-flashing.webp" alt="Wi-Fi connected with flashing yellow status indicator" />
</Frame>

A flashing yellow indicator means your device is connected to the internet but not yet connected to the dashboard. Your device cannot receive commands in this state.

This can happen if:

- **Your device hasn't been paired** — Follow the [pairing guide](/lkbx/quick-start/pairing) to complete setup.
- **Your device just powered on** — Connection typically takes 15–30 seconds, but can take up to 2 minutes depending on your location.

<Warning>
If you've waited more than two minutes and still see the flashing yellow indicator, please contact support as this may indicate a server issue.
</Warning>

#### Status indicator: solid green

<Frame>
  <img src="../../.gitbook/assets/wifi-solid-green.png" alt="Wi-Fi connected with solid green status indicator" />
</Frame>

A solid green indicator means you're online and connected to the dashboard. Your device is ready to receive commands instantly. Have fun!

These icons show your device's current battery level. When your device is charging, you'll see a lightning bolt through the battery icon.

<Warning>
If the battery icon turns red, charge your device immediately to avoid interruption.
</Warning>
