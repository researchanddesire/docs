## Quick reference

### Lock session controls

During an active session, you can:

- **View status** — Check remaining time, lock state, and connectivity on the device display
- **Take breaks** — Pause your session temporarily (if enabled by your keyholder)
- **Emergency unlock** — Access your key immediately (triggers a 24-hour lockout)

<Note>
Your keyholder controls which features are available during your session. Some options may be disabled based on your session settings.
</Note>

### When you need help

If something isn't working as expected:

1. Check the [status icons](./symbols) to diagnose connectivity or lock issues
2. Use the [emergency unlock](./emergency-unlock) if you need immediate access
3. Visit the [support section](/lkbx/support) for troubleshooting guides

<Warning>
Always familiarize yourself with the emergency unlock procedures before starting a session. Having a backup plan ensures you can access your key in any situation.
</Warning>
