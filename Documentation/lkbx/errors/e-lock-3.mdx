---
title: "E-Lock 3: No Backplate Detected"
description: "Troubleshoot the E-Lock 3 error when your Chastity Lockbox cannot detect the backplate during a locking operation."
---

Your Chastity Lockbox is attempting to lock but cannot detect the backplate. This error can occur during any locking operation, including:

- Timed lock sessions
- Permanent locks
- Manual locking
- Locking from the web dashboard

## Why this happens

Your Chastity Lockbox uses two hall effect sensors to detect small magnets embedded in the backplate. When these sensors cannot locate the magnets, the device assumes the backplate is missing and prevents the lock from engaging.

<Info>
This is a safety feature designed to prevent the device from locking without proper backplate attachment.
</Info>

## Common causes

1. **Missing backplate** — The backplate is not attached to your Chastity Lockbox
2. **Misaligned backplate** — The backplate is attached but not properly seated
3. **Debris or contamination** — Dirt or residue is preventing proper contact
4. **Magnetic interference** — Strong magnetic fields nearby are disrupting the sensors
5. **Hardware damage** — The sensors or magnets may be damaged

## How to fix this

<Steps>
<Step title="Verify backplate attachment">
  Confirm the backplate is attached to your Chastity Lockbox. If missing, attach it and ensure it clicks firmly into place.
</Step>

<Step title="Reseat the backplate">
  Remove the backplate completely, then reattach it. Apply gentle pressure until you feel or hear it click into position.
</Step>

<Step title="Clean the connection area">
  Use a dry, lint-free cloth to gently clean both the backplate and the attachment area on the device. Remove any debris, dust, or residue that could interfere with sensor detection.
</Step>

<Step title="Eliminate magnetic interference">
  Move your Chastity Lockbox away from:
  - Electronic devices
  - Speakers or headphones
  - Magnetic mounts or closures
  - Other magnetic objects
  
  Try the locking operation again in a different location.
</Step>

<Step title="Restart the device">
  Remove and reinsert the batteries (or disconnect and reconnect power) to reset the device. Attempt the locking operation again after restart.
</Step>

<Step title="Inspect for physical damage">
  Examine the backplate and attachment area for visible damage, cracks, or deformation. Check that the magnets in the backplate are intact and properly seated.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
If you notice any physical damage to the sensors or magnets, do not attempt to repair them yourself. Contact support for assistance.
</Warning>

## Still having trouble?

If you've tried all the steps above and continue to experience this error, please contact our support team. Include your device serial number and a description of when the error occurs.
