title: "Permalock mode"
description: "Learn how Lock Forever works—an advanced mode that removes time limits and places release entirely in your Keyholder's hands."

Permalock mode (also called "Lock Forever") is an advanced option for the Chastity Lockbox. When you turn it on, all time-based restrictions, counters, and scheduled breaks are removed—leaving release entirely in your Keyholder's control.

<Info>
Lock Forever is an online-only mode. Before using it, connect your device to Wi‑Fi and pair it to your dashboard. See <a href="/chastity-lockbox/quick-start">Quick Start</a> for setup basics.
</Info>

## How it works

When you activate Permalock mode, the Lockbox engages its locking mechanism indefinitely. The device stays locked until one of the following happens:

1. Your Keyholder remotely unlocks the device
2. You use the Emergency Unlock feature
3. The lock is abandoned from the dashboard

<Warning>
There is no preset time limit in this mode. The device does not automatically unlock, and no scheduled breaks or intermissions are provided.
</Warning>

<Note>
Dashboard abandonment is a Keyholder-only action. Self-locks cannot be abandoned. See <a href="/chastity-lockbox/faqs">FAQs</a>.
</Note>

## Safety and communication

Use Permalock mode only after you and your Keyholder agree on responsibilities and emergency procedures.

<Steps>
<Step title="Establish clear communication">
Discuss expectations, boundaries, and how you’ll handle breaks or emergencies before enabling this mode.
</Step>

<Step title="Agree on a safe word or signal">
Choose a specific word or signal that indicates genuine distress—separate from playful resistance.
</Step>

<Step title="Review emergency options together">
Confirm how Emergency Unlock works and when it’s appropriate to use. Share the <a href="/chastity-lockbox/technical-documentation/device-states/emergency-unlock">Emergency Unlock</a> article with your Keyholder.
</Step>

<Step title="Monitor your health">
Regularly check for pain, circulation issues, numbness, or other concerns throughout the lock period.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
You can disable Emergency Unlock from the dashboard while the device is unlocked. If you disable it and later need urgent access, your only option without your Keyholder is the physical backplate break. Learn how in <a href="/chastity-lockbox/support/how-to-do-an-emergency-unlock-by-breaking-the-backplate">Emergency Unlock: Break the Backplate</a>.
</Warning>

## Recommendations for new users

<Tip>
Build up to Permalock mode gradually:

- Start with shorter, timed locks to build trust and experience with your Keyholder
- Use regular timed locks for most play scenarios
- Reserve Permalock mode for special occasions or after you’ve gained significant experience
</Tip>

## Unlocking options

Even in Permalock mode, you still have release options. Choose the safest option that fits your situation.

| Method | Who can use it | When to use it |
|---|---|---|
| Keyholder unlock | Keyholder | Standard release path at any time |
| Dashboard abandonment | Keyholder only | Ends the lock session from the dashboard (not available for self-locks) |
| Emergency Unlock (software) | You | Genuine emergencies when your Keyholder cannot unlock; see <a href="/chastity-lockbox/technical-documentation/device-states/emergency-unlock">Emergency Unlock</a> |
| Emergency Unlock (hardware backplate) | You | Last resort if software Emergency Unlock is disabled or unavailable; see <a href="/chastity-lockbox/support/how-to-do-an-emergency-unlock-by-breaking-the-backplate">Break the Backplate</a> |

<Info>
Always prioritize your physical and mental well‑being. If you feel unsafe or experience genuine distress, use Emergency Unlock. That is what it’s for.
</Info>
