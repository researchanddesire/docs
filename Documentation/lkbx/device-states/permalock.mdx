Permalock mode (also called "Lock Forever") is an advanced feature for users seeking the most intense experience. When activated, all time-based restrictions, counters, and scheduled breaks are disabled—leaving you entirely dependent on your Keyholder for release.

## How it works

When you activate Permalock mode, the Lockbox engages its locking mechanism indefinitely. The device remains locked until one of the following occurs:

1. Your Keyholder remotely unlocks the device
2. You use the emergency unlock feature
3. The lock is abandoned through the dashboard

<Warning>
There is no preset time limit in this mode. The device will not automatically unlock, and no scheduled breaks or intermissions are provided.
</Warning>

## Safety and communication

While Permalock mode offers an extreme level of play, safety must remain the priority.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Establish clear communication">
    Have an open conversation with your Keyholder before engaging this mode. Ensure both parties understand the implications and responsibilities involved.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Agree on a safe word or signal">
    Establish a predetermined safe word or signal that indicates genuine distress—separate from playful resistance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor for health concerns">
    Regularly check for any signs of discomfort, circulation issues, or other health concerns throughout the lock period.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Recommendations for new users

<Tip>
Due to the intense nature of Permalock mode, we strongly recommend building up to it gradually:

- **Start with timed locks** to build trust and experience with your Keyholder
- **Use regular timed locks** for most play scenarios
- **Reserve Permalock mode** for special occasions or after you've gained significant experience
</Tip>

## Unlocking options

Even in Permalock mode, you always have options for release:

| Method | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Keyholder unlock** | Your Keyholder remotely unlocks the device (primary method) |
| **Dashboard abandonment** | The lock can be abandoned through your dashboard |
| **Emergency unlock** | Use the physical emergency release (use with caution) |

<Info>
Always prioritize your physical and mental well-being. If at any point you feel unsafe or experience genuine distress, do not hesitate to use the emergency unlock feature. It exists specifically for situations where your safety is at risk.
</Info>
