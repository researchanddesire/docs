## Before you restart

<Warning>
Do not restart your device during firmware updates unless support instructs you to. Interrupting an update can require manual recovery.
</Warning>

<Note>
Restarting does not cancel active timers or clear your session settings. Your device preserves its locked/unlocked state across restarts. If you need extended battery savings instead, see <a href="/chastity-lockbox/technical-documentation/device-states/sleep-mode">Sleep Mode</a>.
</Note>

## How to restart

<Steps>
<Step title="Use the menu (recommended)">
On the device, navigate to <strong>Settings → Restart Device</strong>, then confirm when prompted. The device will restart automatically.

<Check>
You should see the device power cycle and return to the appropriate screen (lock attempt or main menu) based on its prior state.
</Check>
</Step>

<Step title="Use the hard reset (for unresponsive devices)">
If your device is unresponsive, press and hold the <strong>Enter/Lock</strong> button for <strong>10 seconds</strong>. The screen will go blank and the device will reboot automatically.

<Check>
After the reboot, confirm one of the following:
- If previously locked, the device attempts to re‑engage the lock
- If previously unlocked, the main menu appears
</Check>

<Warning>
Use the hard reset only when the device is unresponsive. For normal restarts, use the menu option.
</Warning>
</Step>
</Steps>

## What to expect after a restart

- Your lock state is preserved
- If locked before restarting — the device automatically attempts to re‑engage the lock
- If unlocked before restarting — you return to the main menu
- Timers and session settings continue without reset

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Device doesn’t power back on">
- Plug into USB power for at least 10 minutes, then hold <strong>Enter/Lock</strong> for 10 seconds
- Try a different USB‑C cable and power source
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Lock didn’t re‑engage after restart">
- Ensure nothing is obstructing the latch
- If you see a calibration prompt, follow the on‑screen steps or visit <a href="/chastity-lockbox/technical-documentation/device-states/calibration">Calibration</a>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Device shows Calibration after restart">
- This can occur after flashing firmware; complete the calibration and the device will resume normal operation
- Learn more at <a href="/chastity-lockbox/technical-documentation/device-states/calibration">Calibration</a>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Device appears offline after restart">
- Reconnect to Wi‑Fi (2.4 GHz only) using the setup flow in <a href="/chastity-lockbox/quick-start">Quick Start</a>
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
