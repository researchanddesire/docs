Your Chastity Lockbox operates in several distinct states, each designed to provide specific functionality while maintaining security and safety. This section covers everything you need to know about how your device behaves in different situations.

## Operational states

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Calibration" icon="sliders" href="./calibration">
    Automatic setup process that configures motor orientation, lock detection, and power settings. Typically pre-configured when you receive your device.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sleep Mode" icon="moon" href="./sleep-mode">
    Power-saving modes that extend battery life during periods of inactivity. Choose between automatic sleep and deep sleep based on your needs.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Lock modes

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Take a Break" icon="clock" href="./take-a-break">
    Scheduled temporary unlocks for hygiene and health maintenance during extended lock periods. Configured by your Keyholder.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Permalock" icon="infinity" href="./permalock">
    Indefinite lock mode with no time restrictions. The device remains locked until your Keyholder grants release.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Safety & maintenance

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Emergency Unlock" icon="triangle-exclamation" href="./emergency-unlock">
    Safety feature for urgent situations when you need immediate access to your keys and your Keyholder is unavailable.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Restarting Device" icon="rotate" href="./restarting">
    How to safely restart your device when needed. Your lock state is preserved through restarts.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Quick reference

| State | Purpose | Keyholder required |
|-------|---------|-------------------|
| Calibration | Initial device setup | No |
| Automatic Sleep | Battery conservation | No |
| Deep Sleep | Extended battery conservation | No |
| Take a Break | Scheduled hygiene breaks | Yes (to configure) |
| Permalock | Indefinite lock | Yes (to release) |
| Emergency Unlock | Safety release | No |

<Warning>
Always prioritize your physical and mental well-being. If you experience genuine distress, use the Emergency Unlock feature without hesitation.
</Warning>

<Note>
Your device preserves its locked or unlocked state through restarts, sleep modes, and power cycles. You never need to worry about accidental state changes.
</Note>
