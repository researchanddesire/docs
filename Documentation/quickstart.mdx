## Get started in three steps

You will pair your device, connect it if needed, and run your first session.

<Info>
Prerequisites

- Charge your device and have a stable 2.4 GHz Wi‑Fi network available for devices that require Wi‑Fi.
- Create your dashboard account and be ready to sign in when prompted.
</Info>

<Steps>
<Step title="Pair or prepare your device">
<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="bullseye-arrow" title="Deepthroat Trainer (DTT)">
    - Pair your trainer with your account using the step‑by‑step guide.
    - Your Trainer ID appears on the device during startup.

    <Card title="Pair your trainer" icon="plug" href="/deepthroat-trainer/quick-start/pairing-your-trainer/step-by-step-guide-for-pairing-your-trainer">
      Follow the pairing walkthrough to connect DTT to your dashboard.
    </Card>

    <Tip>Trainer ID is five or six characters. Do not confuse it with your Wi‑Fi password.</Tip>

    <Check>
    You see the trainer listed in your dashboard devices after pairing.
    </Check>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="gear" title="OSSM (Open Source Sex Machine)">
    - Update or flash firmware if needed, then control OSSM with the RADR wireless remote.

    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="Flash OSSM" icon="bolt" href="/open-source-sex-machine/flashing-your-ossm">
        Use the web flasher if you were instructed to update firmware.
      </Card>
      <Card title="RADR quick start" icon="remote" href="/research-and-desire-remote/quick-start">
        Learn how to power on, pair, and play with RADR.
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>

    <Info>
    Check which devices are supported with RADR in <a href="/research-and-desire-remote/device-support">Device Support</a>.
    </Info>

    <Check>
    RADR shows OSSM controls and responds to speed or pattern changes.
    </Check>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion icon="lock" title="Chastity Lockbox">
    - Start with the Lockbox quick start to connect to Wi‑Fi and learn core actions.

    <Card title="Lockbox quick start" icon="rocket" href="/chastity-lockbox/quick-start">
      Connect to Wi‑Fi, start a lock, and learn emergency options.
    </Card>

    <Warning>
    Only reflash your Lockbox if you are instructed to do so by support. If needed, use <a href="/chastity-lockbox/support/how-to-flash-your-chastity-lockbox">How to flash your Chastity Lockbox</a>.
    </Warning>

    <Check>
    The device connects to your dashboard and lock actions are available.
    </Check>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
</Step>

<Step title="Connect or update as needed">
- Deepthroat Trainer: connect to Wi‑Fi so results can sync to your dashboard.

<Card title="Connect DTT to Wi‑Fi" icon="wifi" href="/deepthroat-trainer/quick-start/pairing-your-trainer/step-by-step-guide-for-connecting-trainer-to-wi-fi">
Complete Wi‑Fi setup from the trainer’s captive portal.
</Card>

- OSSM: ensure the device is flashed to the supported version before using RADR.

<Card title="Flash OSSM" icon="bolt" href="/open-source-sex-machine/flashing-your-ossm">
Follow the flashing guide if you were advised to update.
</Card>

- Chastity Lockbox: finish Wi‑Fi and basic setup in the quick start. Calibration runs automatically after flashing.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Lockbox quick start" icon="rocket" href="/chastity-lockbox/quick-start">
    Wi‑Fi setup, locking, sleep, and emergency options.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Calibration" icon="sliders" href="/chastity-lockbox/technical-documentation/device-states/calibration">
    Understand when calibration appears and what it does.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Check>
Your device shows online status or responds to remote controls from the dashboard or RADR.
</Check>
</Step>

<Step title="Run your first session and review results">
- Deepthroat Trainer: start a training template and review results in your dashboard.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Check your results" icon="chart-line" href="/deepthroat-trainer/quick-start/checking-your-results">
    Verify your grades, points, and leaderboard status.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Explore shareable templates" icon="layers" href="/deepthroat-trainer/training-modes/shareable-templates">
    Browse community templates and add one to your collection.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

- OSSM: use RADR play controls or Pattern Mode to start.

<Card title="RADR quick start" icon="remote" href="/research-and-desire-remote/quick-start">
Power on both devices and follow the pairing and control steps.
</Card>

- Chastity Lockbox: start a lock session, try Sleep when needed, and learn emergency options.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Lockbox quick start" icon="rocket" href="/chastity-lockbox/quick-start">
    Start a lock and manage common actions.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Emergency Unlock" icon="siren" href="/chastity-lockbox/technical-documentation/device-states/emergency-unlock">
    Understand when and how to use Emergency Unlock.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Warning>
Use Emergency Unlock only for genuine emergencies. Some features may temporarily disable locking after use.
</Warning>

<Check>
You completed a first session and can see the outcome in your dashboard.
</Check>
</Step>
</Steps>
