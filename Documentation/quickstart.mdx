## Get started in three steps

Get your Research and Desire devices connected to your Dashboard and complete your first session.

<Steps>
<Step title="Sign in and set your preferences">
  - Create or sign in to your Dashboard account.
  - Open your profile menu and go to Settings to review Account, Communication, and Privacy controls. citeturn2search0

<Check>
  You can see Settings categories like Account Management, Email Preferences, and Privacy Controls.
</Check>

<Tip>
  Use a strong, unique password and review Privacy Controls if you plan to appear on leaderboards.
</Tip>
</Step>

<Step title="Pair your device to the Dashboard">
  - Power on your Deepthroat Trainer and note the Trainer ID shown during boot.
  - In the Dashboard, go to Settings → Devices and enter your Trainer ID exactly as displayed.
  - Select Pair Device to attach it to your account. citeturn1search0

<Warning>
  If the device is already paired to another account, have the original owner unpair it before you proceed.
</Warning>

<Check>
  Your device appears in Settings → Devices as Connected.
</Check>
</Step>

<Step title="Connect your Trainer to Wi‑Fi and run your first session">
  - On the Trainer, hold the button at the startup prompt to open Settings.
  - Connect to the temporary "Trainer Setup" network from your phone.
  - In the captive portal, choose your 2.4 GHz home network, enter the password, and Save. The Trainer will restart and come online. citeturn1search2

<Tip>
  The Trainer uses 2.4 GHz Wi‑Fi only. If your router merges 2.4/5 GHz under one name, create or select a 2.4 GHz SSID for setup.
</Tip>

<Check>
  After restart, your Trainer downloads settings automatically and shows as Online in the Dashboard.
</Check>

  Now start training:
  - Use a built‑in template from Deepthroat Trainer → Templates.
  - Or explore community Shareable Templates and import one if your subscription allows. citeturn2search7

  When you finish a session, view grades, points, and leaderboard placement on the Dashboard Results page. citeturn1search3
</Step>
</Steps>
