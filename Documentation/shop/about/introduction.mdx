## Who We Are

Research And Desire (R+D) is a collective of makers building playful, human-centered hardware and software. We design devices and digital platforms that encourage exploration, skill-building, and connection.

## Our Mission

We create precise, thoughtfully designed tools that help you explore your desires, improve skills through measurable practice, and connect with partners and community. Our approach blends engineering rigor with user-centered design so experiences are both enjoyable and purposeful.

<Tip>
We believe personal technology should inspire curiosity and feel delightfully un-boring.
</Tip>
