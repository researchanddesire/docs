If your Chastity Lockbox or Deepthroat Trainer isn't connecting to Wi-Fi, use this guide to diagnose and resolve the issue.

<Warning>
R+D devices require a **2.4GHz Wi-Fi network**. They are not compatible with 5GHz connections.
</Warning>

<Tip>
Place the device next to your router while troubleshooting. This eliminates weak signal strength as a potential cause.
</Tip>

## Understanding Lockbox Wi-Fi indicators

The Chastity Lockbox displays its connection status through a Wi-Fi icon and a colored circle. Use the following guide to interpret what your device is telling you.

### Wi-Fi icon states

| Icon state | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| Wi-Fi icon **off** | Device is not connected to Wi-Fi |
| Wi-Fi icon **on** (no line through it) | Device may be connected—check the colored circle for details |

## Troubleshooting steps

If your device isn't showing a green circle, work through these steps in order.

<Steps>
<Step title="Restart your router">
Power cycle your router by unplugging it for 30 seconds, then plugging it back in. Once it's fully restarted, reconnect your device to the **2.4GHz** network.
</Step>

<Step title="Verify your Wi-Fi password">
Connect another device (like your phone) to the same 2.4GHz network to confirm the password is correct and the network is functioning.
</Step>

<Step title="Test your internet connection">
Ensure other devices on your network can access the internet. If they can't, the issue is with your network, not your R+D device.
</Step>

<Step title="Try an alternative network">
Connect your device to a different Wi-Fi network, such as a mobile hotspot. If it connects successfully, the issue is with your original network configuration.
</Step>
</Steps>
