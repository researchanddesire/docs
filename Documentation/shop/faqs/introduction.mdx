Find answers to frequently asked questions about our shipping policies, delivery options, and international orders.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is your packaging discreet?">
    Yes, all orders ship in plain, non-descript cardboard boxes with no branding or product information visible on the outside.

    For customs documentation, products are described in generic, safe-for-work terms. For example, the Deepthroat Trainer is listed as a "Wireless Distance Sensor."

    <Card title="View our packaging" icon="box" href="./discreet-shipping">
      See photos of our discreet packaging
    </Card>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Where do orders ship from?">
    All orders ship from our R&D warehouse in Toronto, Ontario, Canada.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Which courier services do you use?">
    We ship via UPS, Canada Post, and ChitChats. ChitChats is a Canadian shipping company that specializes in affordable international delivery, making it our primary carrier for EU and other international shipments.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you ship internationally?">
    Yes, we ship globally wherever our courier partners operate. If you're unsure whether we can deliver to your country, contact us to confirm availability.

    <Card title="Contact us" icon="envelope" href="mailto:support@researchanddesire.com">
      Ask about shipping to your location
    </Card>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Will I pay import or brokerage fees?">
    If you live outside Canada, your shipment may be subject to import duties and/or brokerage fees. Contact your local customs authority for specific information.

    - **North American customers**: Typically no customs or brokerage fees
    - **EU customers**: Brokerage fees average €6.00–8.00
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Will I have to pay VAT?">
    VAT applies to shipments to:
    - European Union countries
    - United Kingdom
    - Switzerland
    - Iceland
    - Norway

    If you're unsure whether VAT applies to your order, check with your local customs authority.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I pay VAT at checkout?">
    Shopify doesn't support VAT collection at checkout. However, if you purchase through our Etsy shop, you can pay VAT at the time of purchase.

    <Note>
      VAT applies to customers in the EU, UK, Switzerland, Iceland, and Norway.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you offer express shipping?">
    Yes! You can choose your preferred shipping speed—express, standard, or other options—during checkout.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long does shipping take?">
    We process most orders within 3–10 business days. Processing time varies based on product availability, customization requirements, and current order volume. If your order is delayed, we'll notify you and offer the option to cancel if you prefer.

    <Info>
      Pre-order items have specific ship dates that depend on the product. Check the product page for estimated delivery timelines.
    </Info>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you ship to P.O. boxes?">
    Yes, we ship to P.O. boxes.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I track my order?">
    When your order ships, you'll receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information. Use the tracking number provided to check your shipment status on the carrier's website (UPS, Canada Post, or ChitChats).

    If you haven't received a shipping confirmation or need help locating your tracking information, contact us and we'll provide your order status.

    <Card title="Contact support" icon="envelope" href="mailto:support@researchanddesire.com">
      Email us with your order number for tracking assistance
    </Card>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I cancel my order?">
    Yes, you can cancel your order before it ships. Email [support@researchanddesire.com](mailto:support@researchanddesire.com) with your order number and we'll process the cancellation.

    Once cancelled, you'll receive a full refund within 5–10 business days.

    <Note>
      If your order has already shipped, contact support to discuss return options.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
