# About Us

### Who We Are

Research And Desire (R+D) is a group of makers that wanted the world to have more creative, playful devices in it. We create hardware and digital platforms that let you play in new ways. We believe technology should serve human connection, exploration, and creativity.

### Our Mission

We develop devices and software that help people explore their desires, improve their skills, and connect with others. Our products combine precision engineering with thoughtful design to create experiences that are both pleasurable and purposeful. Our goal is to make personal technology less boring and more... interesting!

## Our Products

### Deepthroat Trainer (DTT)

Our main IoT device designed for deep throat training and skill development. The DTT features millimeter-precision sensors that measure depth and movement, enabling users to track their progress in reducing gag reflexes, improving breathing techniques, and building endurance. It supports multiple training modes including endurance, repetition, freeform, and bounce sessions. Think of it as a personal trainer, but for your throat. Learn more about it here:&#x20;

### Chastity Lockbox

A device management system that allows users to control access to their devices through customizable lock sessions. The lockbox integrates with our training platform, enabling users to earn time reductions through their training achievements, creating a gamification system that motivates consistent practice and improvement. It's like a video game, but with real-world consequences. Don't worry, we've built in plenty of safety features - we're not monsters.

### Open Source Sex Machine (OSSM)

An advanced mechanical device that provides customizable stimulation patterns through precise stroke control. The OSSM features multiple stroke patterns, adjustable speed and depth controls, and seamless Bluetooth connectivity for remote operation and integration with our digital platform. Yes, we really named it OSSM. We're proud of that one.

## Our Values

**Innovation**: We push the boundaries of what's possible with IoT technology. Sometimes we push too hard and things break, but that's how you learn.

**Safety**: All our products are designed with user safety as the highest priority, featuring built-in safety mechanisms and comprehensive guidelines.&#x20;

**Privacy**: We respect user privacy and implement robust security measures to protect personal data and intimate information. Your secrets are safe with us (and our encryption).

**Community**: We foster a supportive, inclusive community where users can connect, share experiences, and support each other's growth. We're all weirdos here, and that's perfectly fine.

**Quality**: Every product we create meets high standards of engineering, design, and user experience. Our products pass through multiple quality tests before they make their way to you.&#x20;

### Our Approach

We combine cutting-edge technology with human-centered design to create products that truly enhance the human experience. Our team includes engineers, designers, and wellness experts, who work together to ensure our products are safe, effective, and enjoyable to use.&#x20;
