# Keyholder Guide

### Step 1: Connect to your partner's account

1. The Keyolder must first create their own account and sign into it.
2. From the Keyholder's dashboard menu, under the heading `Settings`, select `Partners` .
3. Click "Create an invitation", type in an email address or username, then under Access Type select the "Request access to their devices" and click "Send invitation".
4. You must now sign back into your own account (where the Lockbox was previously paired to) and accept the invitation.

Your Keyholder should now display on your `Settings` >`Partners` page, with the text "This user can control and modify your devices" displayed under their username.

### Step 2: Control lock sessions from your lockee's account

To control someone else's devices as a Keyholder, you must be signed into your own account. Then, click the "Your Account" dropdown in the navigation bar in the top left corner, and select your partner's account.&#x20;

You should now be able to view their current session and stats in the `Chastity Lockbox` >`Home` page, or create new lock sessions for them using the `Chastity Lockbox` > `Templates` page (see "[How to start a lock from the dashboard](how-to-start-a-lock-from-the-dashboard.md)").
