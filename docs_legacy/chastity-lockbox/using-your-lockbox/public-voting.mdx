---
title: "Public Voting"
description: "Let others vote to increase or decrease your lock time for added excitement and community engagement"
---

Public voting lets you share your lock session with others, allowing them to vote on whether your remaining lock time should increase or decrease. This adds an element of unpredictability and community interaction to your chastity experience.

## Enable public voting

<Steps>
<Step title="Open your lockbox settings">
  Navigate to your lockbox settings in the dashboard.
</Step>

<Step title="Enable the public voting option">
  Toggle on **Public Voting** for your lock session.
</Step>

<Step title="Configure time values">
  Set how votes affect your lock time:
  - **Time Plus**: Seconds added when someone votes to increase
  - **Time Minus**: Seconds removed when someone votes to decrease
</Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Consider setting moderate time increments to avoid extreme changes to your lock duration.
</Tip>

## Share your voting link

Once public voting is enabled for an active lock session:

<Steps>
<Step title="Go to your lockbox page">
  Open your lockbox page in the dashboard.
</Step>

<Step title="Find the Public Voting badge">
  Look for the **Public Voting** badge with a share icon.
</Step>

<Step title="Copy and share your link">
  Click the badge to copy your unique public voting link, then share it with friends, partners, or your community.
</Step>
</Steps>

## View voting activity

You can track all votes on your lock session in two places.

### Vote counter badges

On your lockbox page, vote counter badges appear next to your lock session details:

- **👍 Thumbs Up**: Total votes to increase your lock time
- **👎 Thumbs Down**: Total votes to decrease your lock time

<Note>
These badges only appear when public voting is enabled or when your session has received at least one vote.
</Note>

### Activity history

For detailed vote information:

1. Navigate to your lockbox page
2. Find the **Session activity** section showing recent events
3. Click **View more** to open the full activity log

Each vote event displays:
- **Icon**: 👍 for increase votes, 👎 for decrease votes
- **Action**: "Voted to increase time" or "Voted to decrease time"
- **Voter**: Username of the voter (or "Anonymous" if not logged in)
- **Timestamp**: When the vote was cast

Vote events appear chronologically alongside other session activities like locks, unlocks, and breaks.

## Voting restrictions

To prevent abuse, the following restrictions apply:

- Each person can vote once every 24 hours on your session
- Votes are tracked by both IP address and user account

## Privacy and anonymity

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Logged-in voters">
When someone votes while logged into their account, their username appears in your activity history.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Anonymous voters">
When someone votes without logging in, they appear as "Anonymous" in your activity history.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Your privacy">
Your public voting link does not reveal personal information beyond your session details.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Points and rewards

Both you and voters earn points for participation:

| Participant | Reward |
|-------------|--------|
| You | Earn points when people vote on your session |
| Voters | Earn points when they vote on sessions |

<Info>
Point values decrease throughout the day as more votes are cast, encouraging early participation.
</Info>

## Tips for engagement

- Share your link on social media or community forums for maximum participation
- Check your activity history regularly to see who's supporting or challenging you
- Remember that votes affect your lock time in real-time—share responsibly

## Disable public voting

You can disable public voting at any time:

<Steps>
<Step title="Open session settings">
  Go to your lock session settings.
</Step>

<Step title="Turn off public voting">
  Toggle off the **Public Voting** option.
</Step>
</Steps>

Your public link will stop accepting new votes, but previous votes remain in your history.

<Warning>
Public voting requires an active lock session. If your session ends or is abandoned, the voting link becomes inactive.
</Warning>
