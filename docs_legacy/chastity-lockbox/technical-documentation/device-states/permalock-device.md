# Permalock Device

## Overview

The "Lock Forever" mode is an extreme feature of the Chastity Lockbox designed for users seeking an intense and prolonged experience. This mode removes all time-based restrictions, counters, and scheduled breaks, leaving the Lockee entirely dependent on their Keyholder for release.

### How It Works

When activated, the Lock Forever mode engages the Lockbox's locking mechanism indefinitely. The device will remain locked until one of the following occurs:

1. The Keyholder remotely unlocks the device
2. The Lockee uses the emergency unlock feature
3. The lock is abandoned through the dashboard

### Important Considerations

- There is no preset time limit in this mode
- The device will not automatically unlock
- No scheduled breaks or intermissions are provided

### Safety and Precautions

While the Lock Forever mode offers an extreme level of play, it's crucial to prioritize safety:

1. Establish clear communication with your Keyholder before engaging this mode
2. Ensure both parties understand the implications and responsibilities
3. Have a predetermined safe word or signal to indicate genuine distress
4. Regularly check for any signs of discomfort or health concerns

### Recommendations

Due to the intense nature of the Lock Forever mode, we strongly recommend:

- Starting with shorter, timed locks to build trust and experience
- Using regular timed locks for most play scenarios
- Reserving the Lock Forever mode for special occasions or experienced users

### Unlocking Options

Remember, even in Lock Forever mode, you have options:

1. Keyholder remote unlock (primary method)
2. Dashboard abandonment
3. Emergency unlock feature (use with caution)

Always prioritize your physical and mental well-being. If at any point you feel unsafe or overly distressed, do not hesitate to use the emergency unlock feature.
