# Take a Break

### Overview

The "Break" option is a crucial feature of the Chastity Lockbox, designed to allow for necessary hygiene and health maintenance during extended lock periods. This feature can be accessed when your device is locked by entering the lock menu (hold down the right button).

### How It Works

1. **Configuration**: Breaks are configured in your online dashboard by your Keyholder. They set the frequency and duration of breaks.
   - Example: A 15-minute break every 2 days.
2. **Activation**: When you start a break, a countdown timer begins.
3. **Break Period**: During this time, you should:
   - Unlock the device
   - Attend to hygiene needs
   - Re-lock the device
   - Return the key to the Lockbox
4. **Completion**: The break ends when you re-lock the device or when the timer runs out, whichever comes first.

### Important Considerations

- The timer starts as soon as you activate the break feature.
- You must complete all necessary actions before the timer expires.
- Failing to re-lock before the timer ends may result in penalties.

### Penalties for Overdue Locks

If you do not re-lock the device before the break timer expires:

1. **With 'Shame' Enabled**:
   - Your missed lock time will be shared publicly.
   - The public can vote on your lock conditions.
2. **Without 'Shame' Enabled**:
   - Your Keyholder will be notified of the missed lock time.
   - Any punishment will be at your Keyholder's discretion.

### Best Practices

- Plan your breaks carefully to ensure you have enough time.
- Set up a routine to efficiently use your break time.
- Communicate with your Keyholder if you anticipate any issues with scheduled breaks.

Remember, the break feature is designed for your health and safety. Use it responsibly to maintain hygiene while respecting the rules set by your Keyholder.
