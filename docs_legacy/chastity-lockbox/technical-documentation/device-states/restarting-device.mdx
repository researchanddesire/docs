## How to restart

You can restart your device using either of the following methods:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Button method">
    Press and hold the **Lock/Enter button** for 7 seconds. The device will restart automatically.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Menu method">
    Navigate to **Settings → Restart Device** and confirm your selection.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## What happens after a restart

Your device preserves its state across restarts:

- **If locked before restarting** — The device will automatically attempt to re-engage the lock
- **If unlocked before restarting** — You'll return to the main menu

<Warning>
Do not restart your device during firmware updates unless specifically instructed by support. Interrupting an update can cause issues that require manual recovery.
</Warning>

## Need help?

If you experience any issues during or after a restart, contact our support team at [support@researchanddesire.com](mailto:support@researchanddesire.com).
