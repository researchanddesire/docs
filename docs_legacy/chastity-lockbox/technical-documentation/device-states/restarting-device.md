# Restarting Device

If you need to restart your device, follow these simple steps:

- Press and hold both the **Lock/enter button for 7 seconds**.

OR

- Navigate to settings -> restart device

The device will restart automatically!

### What happens after restart:

- If your device was locked before restarting, it will attempt to lock again.
- If your device was unlocked, you'll see the main menu after restart.

**Important:** Do not restart your device during updates unless specifically instructed to do so by [support@researchanddesire.com](mailto:support@researchanddesire.com).

For any questions or concerns, please contact our support team <3
