# Sleep Mode

### Understanding Sleep Modes

Your Chastity Lockbox has two types of sleep modes to help conserve battery life:

1. Automatic Sleep
2. Deep Sleep (Manual activation required)

#### Automatic Sleep

This mode activates automatically to save power during short periods of inactivity:

- The screen will dim or turn off
- Some non-essential functions may be temporarily disabled
- WiFi remains connected, allowing communication with keyholders
- Suitable for short power breaks (e.g., commuting to and from work)

#### Deep Sleep

This mode offers maximum power saving but requires manual activation:

- Activated by selecting 'SLEEP' from the menu and confirming with a long press on the enter button
- Turns off the screen, WiFi, Bluetooth, and most sensors
- The device maintains its locked/unlocked state
- WiFi and remote communication are disabled
- Wake up by pressing the left (back) button

### When to Use Deep Sleep

Use Deep Sleep only when you need to conserve power for extended periods, such as:

- During long trips where you might need to unlock midway
- When you won't have access to a power source for an extended time

### Important Notes

1. Deep Sleep is optional and must be activated manually
2. When using Deep Sleep, you won't receive updates or commands from keyholders
3. For regular daily use, the automatic sleep mode is usually sufficient
4. We recommend keeping your device plugged in when at home

Remember: Deep Sleep should only be used when necessary. In most cases, the automatic power-saving features will be enough to maintain your device's battery life while keeping you connected.
