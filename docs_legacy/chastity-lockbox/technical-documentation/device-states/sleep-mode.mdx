## Overview

Your Chastity Lockbox includes two sleep modes to help conserve battery life while maintaining the functionality you need.

| Mode | Activation | WiFi | Best For |
|------|------------|------|----------|
| Automatic Sleep | Automatic after inactivity | Connected | Daily use |
| Deep Sleep | Manual via menu | Disabled | Extended periods without power |

## Automatic Sleep

Automatic sleep activates on its own during short periods of inactivity. You don't need to do anything—the device handles power management intelligently.

**What happens in automatic sleep:**
- The screen dims or turns off
- Non-essential functions pause temporarily
- WiFi stays connected for keyholder communication
- The device wakes instantly when you interact with it

<Tip>
Automatic sleep is ideal for daily routines like commuting or working. Your keyholder can still send commands while the device conserves power.
</Tip>

## Deep Sleep

Deep sleep provides maximum power savings but requires you to activate it manually. Use this mode when you need to preserve battery for extended periods.

**What happens in deep sleep:**
- Screen, WiFi, Bluetooth, and most sensors turn off
- The locked or unlocked state remains unchanged
- Remote communication with keyholders is disabled
- The device uses minimal power

<Steps>
<Step title="Enter deep sleep">
Navigate to the menu and select **SLEEP**, then long-press the enter button to confirm.
</Step>

<Step title="Wake the device">
Press the left (back) button to exit deep sleep and restore full functionality.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
While in deep sleep, you won't receive updates or commands from your keyholder. Only use this mode when you're certain you won't need remote access.
</Warning>

## When to Use Deep Sleep

Deep sleep is appropriate for specific situations where power conservation takes priority over connectivity:

- **Long trips** where you might need to unlock midway but won't have charging access
- **Extended time away from power** such as camping or travel
- **Storage** when not using the device for several days

<Note>
For regular daily use, automatic sleep handles power management effectively. Most users never need to activate deep sleep manually.
</Note>

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Maximize battery life">
- Keep the device plugged in when you're at home
- Use automatic sleep for daily routines
- Reserve deep sleep for situations where you genuinely need it
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Stay connected to your keyholder">
- Avoid deep sleep if your keyholder may need to send commands
- Communicate with your keyholder before entering deep sleep
- Remember that automatic sleep maintains full connectivity
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
