### What is Calibration?

Calibration is an automatic process that helps your Chastity Lockbox understand how it's assembled and how it should function. It's a crucial step in ensuring your device works correctly and safely.

### When Does Calibration Happen?

Good news! In most cases, you don't need to worry about calibration at all. Here's what you need to know:

1. **Pre-Calibrated Devices**: Your Chastity Lockbox typically comes pre-calibrated when you receive it. This means it's ready to use right out of the box!
2. **After Flashing**: If you've recently updated your device's software (a process called "flashing"), you might encounter the calibration page. Don't worry - this is normal and only happens once after a software update.

### What Does Calibration Do?

While you don't need to perform the calibration yourself, it's good to know what it does:

1. **Motor Orientation**: Determines which way the motor should turn to lock and unlock.
2. **Lock Detection**: Teaches the Lockbox how to know when it's securely locked.
3. **Motor Power Setting**: Sets the optimal power level for the motor to ensure secure locking without excessive battery drain.

### What If I See the Calibration Page?

If you do encounter the calibration page:

- Don't panic! This is normal, especially after a software update.
- The process is automatic and should complete on its own.
- Once finished, you shouldn't see this page again unless you update the device's software.

### Need Help?

If you're having any issues with your device or have questions about the calibration process, don't hesitate to reach out to customer support or your Keyholder for assistance.

Remember, the Chastity Lockbox is designed to be user-friendly and secure. The automatic calibration is just one of the ways we ensure your experience is smooth and worry-free!
