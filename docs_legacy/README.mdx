## Who We Are

Research And Desire (R+D) is a collective of makers who believe the world needs more creative, playful devices. We build hardware and digital platforms that let you play in new ways. Our philosophy is simple: technology should serve human connection, exploration, and creativity.

## Our Mission

We develop devices and software that help people explore their desires, improve their skills, and connect with others. Our products combine precision engineering with thoughtful design to create experiences that are both pleasurable and purposeful.

<Tip>
Our goal is to make personal technology less boring and more... interesting.
</Tip>
