# OSSM PCB Spec sheet

Input voltage: 24V up to 5A max

\*\*Please use a high quality UL certified supply, low quality power supplies often cannot output their rated power and can cause damage to motor or board.

Output signals

- Step-Dir outputs @5V active low
- PH-4 connector

Output Power to motor

- 24V up to 6A
- 2x screw terminals - 16-24AWG

Back-EMF protection:&#x20;

- Inline diode protects power supply
- 1500uF capacitor handles regen up to 50V
