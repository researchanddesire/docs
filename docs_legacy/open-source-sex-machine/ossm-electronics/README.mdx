## Key specifications at a glance

| Component | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| Input voltage | 24V DC (up to 5A) |
| Motor output | 24V up to 6A |
| Signal type | Step-Dir @ 5V active low |
| Connector types | PH-4 (signal), screw terminals (power) |

<Warning>
Always use a high-quality, UL-certified power supply. Low-quality power supplies often cannot deliver their rated power and may damage your motor or board.
</Warning>

## Before you begin

Make sure you have the following before starting any electronics work:

- OSSM PCB (control board)
- OSSM Gold Motor (57AIM30) or compatible servo motor
- 24V power supply (minimum 5A, UL-certified recommended)
- Basic tools: small flat head screwdriver, wire strippers, snippers

<Note>
If you purchased your motor from Research and Desire, your kit includes all necessary cables and connectors.
</Note>
