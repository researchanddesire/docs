Your OSSM needs a stable, secure mounting system to operate safely and effectively. The right mount depends on your use case, available space, and how much adjustability you need.

## Available mounts

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Basic Mount V2" icon="box" href="ossm-basic-mount-v2">
    A compact, printed mounting solution ideal for getting started quickly. Perfect for users who want simplicity without sacrificing stability.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Extrusion Mount" icon="ruler-combined" href="ossm-extrusion-mount">
    An adjustable aluminum extrusion-based system with multiple pivot points. Best for users who need maximum flexibility in positioning and angle adjustment.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Choosing the right mount

| Feature | Basic Mount V2 | Extrusion Mount |
|---------|---------------|-----------------|
| **Adjustability** | Fixed position | Fully adjustable |
| **Footprint** | Compact | Larger |
| **Assembly time** | Quick | Moderate |
| **Best for** | Simple setups | Versatile positioning |

<Tip>
If you're unsure which mount to choose, start with the Basic Mount V2. You can always upgrade to the Extrusion Mount later as your needs evolve.
</Tip>

## Safety considerations

<Warning>
Always ensure your mount is securely fastened before use. A stable mounting system is essential for safe operation – never use your OSSM with an unsecured or wobbly mount.
</Warning>

- Place your mount on a flat, stable surface
- Use the included grippy feet or sandbag (Extrusion Mount) for added stability
- Check all bolts and connections before each use
- Never exceed the recommended load capacity for your mount type
