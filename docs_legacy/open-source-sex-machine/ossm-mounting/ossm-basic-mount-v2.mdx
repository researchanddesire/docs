## Getting started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Unbox your kit">
    Verify all components are present by checking against the bill of materials below. If anything is missing, contact support before proceeding.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your OSSM board">
    Visit [ossm.tech](https://ossm.tech) for detailed connection instructions and firmware setup guides.
    
    <Tip>
      Join the community Discord for real-time help and to connect with other builders.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assemble and explore">
    Follow the assembly instructions to build your mount, then enjoy creating your own exploration tools.
  </Step>
</Steps>
