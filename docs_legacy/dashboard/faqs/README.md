# FAQs

### How can I create a dashboard account?

To create a dashboard account, head to our [dashboard website](https://dashboard.researchanddesire.com/app). Once there, click "Create a free account" and you will be prompted to input your email address and choose a password for your account. Then click the blue "Create account" button and you will receive an account confirmation link via email. Navigate to your email account, confirm by clicking the hyperlink in the email, and you’re all set! 👌

If for any reason you are unable to make a dashboard account, the please let us know immediately.

### How can I reset my password?

To reset your dashboard account password, navigate to the Settings>My Profile page. Then, scroll to the bottom of the page and click the “Change Password” button. You will be prompted to enter your new password and verify it by clicking the “Reset Password” button. 🔑

### Good heavens, I’ve forgotten my password! How can I recover my account?

Nothing to worry about! We’ve made account recovery both secure and simple 🤓. To recover your account, head to the dashboard sign-in page and enter just your email. Then, click the “Email link” option to receive an email with both a magic link and unique sign-in code. You have the option to sign into your account via the URL provided in the email or via entry of the code on our dashboard. Once signed in, you can change your password by following the steps in the “How can I reset my password?” FAQ. 👌
