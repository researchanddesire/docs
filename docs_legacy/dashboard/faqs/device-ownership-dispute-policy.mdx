Devices such as the **Deep Throat Trainer** and **Chastity Lockbox** are often shared between users—typically in dynamics involving a "top" and a "bottom." Occasionally, disputes arise regarding device ownership, particularly when both parties have accessed the device through their respective accounts.

This policy explains how we determine ownership and resolve disputes.

## Primary ownership

**The primary owner of a device is the individual who completed the purchase.**

- If we can verify a purchase through a Shopify order or internal store records, **and the purchaser's email matches the one used to contact us**, they are the device owner.
- The primary owner can **request the device be unpaired from any account**, regardless of which account currently holds it.
- No justification is required. Upon a verified request, **support will immediately unpair the device.**

## Ownership without a purchase record

When no purchase record exists, we establish ownership based on **device usage history**:

- The **first account** to log an event from the device (such as pairing, usage, or commands) becomes the **initial owner**.
- This account holder may request the device be unpaired from any account at any time, for any reason.
- Support staff will process unpairing requests immediately.

## Transferring ownership

Ownership can transfer under the following circumstances:

### Voluntary unpairing by the owner

- When the current owner **unpairs the device from their account**, this constitutes an intentional release of ownership.
- The next user to pair the device becomes the **new owner**.
- Once a new user pairs the device after an unpairing event, **ownership transfers to that new user**.

<Warning>
**Unpairing relinquishes ownership.** Only unpair your device if you are prepared to give up control of it permanently.
</Warning>

<Info>
When ownership history is unclear, Research and Desire assigns ownership to the original purchaser.
</Info>

### Transfer of ownership by Research and Desire

Sometimes a user requests a device transfer while the original purchaser remains the listed owner. If the device is **still paired to the original purchaser's account** and they have **not unpaired it**, the following process applies:

1. **Research and Desire contacts the purchaser** at the email address associated with the original order.
2. The purchaser has **30 days to respond** to the transfer request.
3. If the purchaser **approves the transfer**, we immediately transfer ownership to the requesting user.
4. If the purchaser **declines the transfer**, we **do not** transfer the device.
5. If the purchaser **does not respond within 30 days**, we consider them non-responsive and **transfer the device to the requesting user**.

<Info>
This process respects the rights of original purchasers while providing a clear path forward for active users in unresolved disputes.
</Info>
