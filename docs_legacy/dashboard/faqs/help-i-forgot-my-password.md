# Help! I forgot my password!

#### If you forgot your dashboard password, you can still login using "Email link"

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-09-10 084944.jpg" alt="" width="283"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

You will receive an email with further instructions!

### How to Reset Your Password

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2025-09-10 085132.jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

To reset your dashboard account password, navigate to the Settings>My Profile page. Then, scroll to the bottom of the page and click the “Change Password” button. You will be prompted to enter your new password and verify it by clicking the “Reset Password” button. 🔑
