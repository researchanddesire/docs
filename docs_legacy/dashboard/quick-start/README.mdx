Get up and running with the Research and Desire Dashboard. Follow these steps to create your account and begin exploring.

## Create your account

<Steps>
<Step title="Visit the Dashboard">
  Go to the [Research and Desire Dashboard](https://dashboard.researchanddesire.com).
</Step>

<Step title="Sign up">
  Click **Create a free account** at the bottom of the login page. You can sign up using your email and a password, or use a social provider for faster access.
</Step>

<Step title="Verify your email">
  Check your inbox for a verification email and click the confirmation link to activate your account.

  <Tip>
  If you don't see the email, check your spam folder or request a new verification link.
  </Tip>
</Step>

<Step title="Complete onboarding">
  Follow the onboarding flow to configure your account preferences and get familiar with the dashboard.

  <Check>
  Once onboarding is complete, you're ready to start using the platform.
  </Check>
</Step>
</Steps>

## What's next?

After creating your account, explore the dashboard to discover all available features. If you need help along the way, check out the rest of our documentation or reach out to support.
