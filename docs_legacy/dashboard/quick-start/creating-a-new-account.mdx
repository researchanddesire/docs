## Get started in minutes

Creating your account takes just a few steps and gives you full control over your devices from any browser or mobile device.

<Steps>
<Step title="Navigate to the dashboard">
  Visit [researchanddesire.com](https://www.researchanddesire.com) and click **Login** in the top right corner. This takes you to the dashboard at `dashboard.researchanddesire.com`.
</Step>

<Step title="Register for an account">
  On the sign-in screen, click **Register** at the bottom. Enter your email address and create a secure password.

  <Tip>
  You can also sign up using one of our social login options for faster access.
  </Tip>
</Step>

<Step title="Confirm your email">
  After submitting the registration form, check your inbox for a confirmation email from `app@researchanddesire.com`. Click the link in the email to verify your account.
</Step>

<Step title="Complete onboarding">
  Follow the guided onboarding flow to configure the basics of your account. Once complete, you're ready to start controlling your devices.

  <Check>
  Your account is now set up and ready to use.
  </Check>
</Step>
</Steps>
