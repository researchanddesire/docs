## Overview

Manage your email notification preferences in the [Research and Desire dashboard](https://dashboard.researchanddesire.com). You can enable or disable all email notifications globally, or fine-tune specific notification categories.

## Accessing Email Settings

{% stepper %}
{% step %}

#### Navigate to Settings

Go to [dashboard.researchanddesire.com](https://dashboard.researchanddesire.com) and click **Settings** in the navigation menu.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}

#### Open Advanced Settings

Click **Advanced** within the Settings section.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}

#### Locate Email Settings

Find the **Email Settings** section in the middle of the page.
{% endstep %}
{% endstepper %}

## Email Controls

### Global Toggle

Use the main toggle to enable or disable **all** email notifications from Research and Desire with a single click.

### Test Email

Click **Send Test Email** to verify your settings are working correctly. Check your inbox for a confirmation email.

## Notification Categories

### Leaderboard Events

- Promotion/demotion zone notifications
- Significant rank changes

### Lockbox Events

- Lock/unlock events
- Schedule changes
- Partner activity updates

### Trainer Events

- Training session starts
- Training mode changes
- Device connection status

### Sharing Events

- Play invitations
- Partner connection requests

### Marketing Emails

- Promotional codes and discounts
- New product announcements

## Troubleshooting

If you're not receiving expected notifications:

1. Check that the main email toggle is enabled
2. Verify specific notification categories are enabled
3. Use the test email feature to confirm settings
4. Check your spam folder
5. Verify your email address is correct
