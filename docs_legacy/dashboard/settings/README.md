# Dashboard Settings

Configure your account preferences, privacy settings, and personal information through the dashboard settings.

## Available Settings

### Account Management

- **[Account Settings](account-settings.md)** - Change your email address and password
- **[Email Preferences](email-preferences.md)** - Configure notification and communication preferences

### Privacy & Security

- **[Public Links Opt-Out](public-links-opt-out.md)** - Control your visibility in public leaderboards and sharing

## Accessing Settings

To access your dashboard settings:

1. **Sign in** to your dashboard account
2. **Click your profile picture** in the top-right corner
3. **Select "Profile"** from the dropdown menu
4. **Navigate** through the different settings sections

## Settings Categories

### Private Details

Manage your personal account information including:

- Email address changes
- Password updates
- Account security settings

### Communication Preferences

Control how you receive notifications and updates:

- Email notification settings
- Communication frequency preferences
- Marketing email opt-ins/opt-outs

### Privacy Controls

Manage your visibility and data sharing:

- Public profile settings
- Leaderboard participation
- Data sharing preferences

## Getting Help

If you need assistance with any settings:

1. **Check the specific guide** for the setting you're trying to configure
2. **Review the troubleshooting sections** in each guide
3. **Contact support** if you encounter persistent issues

## Security Notes

- **Always verify email changes** through the confirmation link sent to your new email
- **Use strong, unique passwords** for better account security
- **Review your privacy settings** regularly to ensure they match your preferences
- **Keep your contact information up to date** to receive important account notifications
