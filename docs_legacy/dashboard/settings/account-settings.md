# Account Settings

Manage your account credentials and personal information through the dashboard settings.

## Overview

Your account settings allow you to:

* **Change your email address** - Update the email associated with your account
* **Change your password** - Update your account password for better security

Both features are accessible through the **Private Details** section in your profile settings.

## Changing Your Email Address

### Step-by-Step Guide

1. **Navigate to Profile Settings**
   * Click on your profile picture in the top-right corner
   * Select "Profile" from the dropdown menu
   * You'll see the "Private Details" section
2. **Open Email Change Dialog**
   * In the "Email Address" section, click the "Change Email" button
   * A dialog box will appear with your current email address
3. **Enter New Email Address**
   * Type your new email address in the "New Email Address" field
   * Make sure the email is different from your current one
   * The system will validate the email format automatically
4. **Submit the Change**
   * Click "Update Email" to submit your request
   * You'll see a loading message while the change is processed
5. **Email Verification Required**
   * After submitting, you'll be automatically logged out
   * You'll be redirected to a confirmation page
   * Check your new email inbox for a verification link
   * Click the verification link to complete the email change
6. **Sign Back In**
   * Once verified, you can sign back in with your new email address
   * Your old email address will no longer work for login \{% endstepper %\}

### Important Notes

{% hint style="info" %}
**Email Verification Required**: You must verify your new email address before you can sign in again. If you don't receive the verification email within a few minutes, check your spam folder.
{% endhint %}

* **Email Uniqueness**: The new email address must not already be in use by another account
* **Automatic Logout**: You'll be logged out immediately after requesting the change for security reasons
* **Verification Time**: The verification link is typically sent within seconds
* **No Undo**: Once verified, you cannot revert to your old email address without going through the change process again

### Troubleshooting Email Changes

**Didn't receive the verification email?**

* Check your spam/junk folder
* Wait a few minutes and check again
* Make sure you entered the correct email address
* Contact support if the issue persists

**Can't access the new email?**

* If you made a typo in the email address, you'll need to contact support
* The old email address will not work for login after the change

## Changing Your Password

### Step-by-Step Guide

1. **Navigate to Password Settings**
   * Go to your profile settings (Profile → Private Details)
   * Click the "Change Password" button in the Password section
   * You'll be taken to a dedicated password change page
2. **Enter New Password**
   * Type your new password in the "New Password" field
   * The system will validate your password in real-time
   * Use the info icon (ℹ️) to see password requirements
3. **Meet Password Requirements**
   * At least 8 characters long
   * One uppercase letter (A-Z)
   * One lowercase letter (a-z)
   * One number (0-9)
   * One special character (!@#$%^&\* etc.)
4. **Submit the Change**
   * Click "Reset Password" to update your password
   * You'll see a loading message while the change is processed
   * Upon success, you'll be redirected back to the main dashboard
5. **Sign In with New Password**
   * Your new password is immediately active
   * Use the new password for all future logins
   * Update any saved passwords in your browser or password manager \{% endstepper %\}

### Password Security Tips

{% hint style="info" %}
**Strong Password Best Practices**:

* Use a unique password that you don't use elsewhere
* Consider using a password manager to generate and store secure passwords
* Avoid using personal information like names or birthdays
* Make it memorable but complex
{% endhint %}

* **Immediate Effect**: Password changes take effect immediately
* **No Verification Required**: Unlike email changes, password changes don't require email verification
* **Security**: Your old password becomes invalid as soon as the new one is set
* **Sessions**: You may need to sign in again on other devices

### Troubleshooting Password Changes

**Password not meeting requirements?**

* Check that all requirements are met (length, case, numbers, special characters)
* The system will show specific error messages for each requirement
* Try a different combination that meets all criteria

**Can't remember your new password?**

* Use the "Forgot Password" feature on the login page
* This will send a password reset link to your email
* You can then set a new password through the reset process

## Security Considerations

### Email Changes

* **Verification Required**: Email changes require verification to prevent unauthorized access
* **Account Security**: Changing your email helps protect against account takeovers
* **Communication**: All future emails from the system will go to your new address

### Password Changes

* **Regular Updates**: Consider changing your password periodically for better security
* **Unique Passwords**: Never reuse passwords from other accounts
* **Two-Factor Authentication**: Consider enabling additional security measures if available

## Getting Help

If you encounter any issues with changing your email or password:

1. **Check the troubleshooting sections** above for common solutions
2. **Verify your internet connection** is stable
3. **Try refreshing the page** and attempting the change again
4. **Contact support** if problems persist

{% hint style="info" %}
**Support Contact**: If you need assistance, reach out through the support channels available in your dashboard or contact the support team directly.
{% endhint %}
