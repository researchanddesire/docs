# Points

Whether you're just starting out or you're a seasoned trainer, this guide will help you understand how to earn points and climb the ranks.

### What Are Training Points? ✨

Training points are your reward for dedication and progress! Every time you complete a training session or engage with the community, you'll earn these shiny points that showcase your achievements. The more points you earn, the higher you'll climb on our global leaderboards, where you can see how you stack up against trainers worldwide!

### Earning Your Points 🎯

There are two awesome ways to rack up those points:

#### 1. Training Sessions

Jump into any of our training modes and start earning! Each successful session adds to your point total. The better you perform, the more points you'll earn!

#### 2. Community Engagement

Share your lockbox links publicly and let the community show their support! Every vote you receive adds to your point total - it's our way of rewarding those who actively participate in our wonderful community.

### Let's Break Down the Training Modes! 🎮

#### Repetition Mode

Think of this as your rhythm master! Each repetition counts, and when you nail that perfect accuracy, your points will soar! The formula is simple: your reps × 2 × how accurate you were. Keep that rhythm steady!

#### Bounce Mode

Just like Repetition Mode, but with a bouncy twist! Same scoring, same fun - it's all about finding your groove and maintaining that perfect rhythm.

#### Endurance Mode

This is where your stamina shines! The longer you go and the deeper you train, the more points you'll earn. We take your time (in seconds), divide it by 2, and multiply it by your depth level. Push your limits and watch those points climb!

#### Freeform Mode

Express yourself! This creative mode rewards both your timing and your style. Take your time (in seconds), divide by 2, and multiply by how well you kept to the rhythm. It's the perfect blend of creativity and precision!

### Climbing the Leaderboards 🏆

Your points are your ticket to fame! Watch as you climb through the rankings on our:

- All-time leaderboard (for the history books!)
- Monthly rankings (new chances every month!)
- Weekly charts (perfect for short-term goals!)

### Tips for Success! 💫

- Remember: slow and steady wins the race! Focus on accuracy rather than speed
- Make training a regular part of your routine
- Mix up your training modes to keep things fresh and fun
- Share your journey with the community - those votes add up!
- Set personal goals and celebrate every milestone
- Check the leaderboards for inspiration and friendly competition

Most importantly, have fun with it! Every point you earn is a celebration of your progress, and every position you climb on the leaderboard is proof of your dedication. Keep training, keep smiling, and keep reaching for those stars! 🌟

Remember, we're all on this journey together, and every point you earn is a step forward in your personal training adventure! Happy training! 🎉
