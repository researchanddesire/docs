## Understanding the leveling system

The leveling system draws inspiration from RuneScape's iconic progression model. You start at Level 1 and can advance all the way to Level 120 through accumulated points.

### How progression works

- You begin at Level 1
- Earn points through various activities
- Accumulate points to increase your level
- Reach a maximum level of 120

<Tip>
The exponential growth curve means early levels come quickly, while higher levels represent true dedication and mastery.
</Tip>

### Key features

- **Exponential progression** — Each level requires more points than the previous one, creating meaningful advancement
- **Long-term goals** — Reaching Level 120 requires significant dedication and consistent achievement
- **Meaningful milestones** — Every 10 levels marks a major accomplishment in your journey

## Progression tiers

The points required for each level follow an exponential growth pattern. This creates a rewarding system where advancement becomes progressively more challenging:

| Tier | Level Range | Difficulty |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Beginner | 1–20 | Quick to achieve |
| Intermediate | 21–60 | Requires consistent effort |
| Advanced | 61–100 | Represents significant achievement |
| Master | 101–120 | Reserved for the most dedicated players |

<Note>
The exponential curve ensures that each new level feels like a genuine accomplishment, especially as you approach the higher tiers.
</Note>
