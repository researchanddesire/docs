Training points reward your dedication and progress. Every completed session and community interaction earns points that appear on our global leaderboards, where you can compare your achievements with trainers worldwide.

## How to earn points

You can accumulate points through two primary methods:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Training sessions" icon="dumbbell">
    Complete any training mode to earn points. Your performance directly affects how many points you receive.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Community engagement" icon="users">
    Share your lockbox links publicly and receive votes from other community members. Each vote adds to your point total.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Points by training mode

Each training mode uses a different formula to calculate your points based on your performance metrics.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Repetition Mode">
    Points reward both your rep count and precision.

    **Formula:** `repetitions × 2 × accuracy`

    Focus on maintaining steady rhythm for maximum accuracy multiplier.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Bounce Mode">
    Uses the same scoring system as Repetition Mode with identical mechanics.

    **Formula:** `repetitions × 2 × accuracy`

    Find your groove and maintain consistent rhythm throughout the session.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Endurance Mode">
    Rewards sustained effort and training depth over time.

    **Formula:** `(time in seconds ÷ 2) × depth level`

    Push your limits—longer sessions at deeper levels yield significantly more points.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Freeform Mode">
    Balances creative expression with rhythmic precision.

    **Formula:** `(time in seconds ÷ 2) × rhythm accuracy`

    Take your time while maintaining consistent timing for the best results.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Leaderboard rankings

Your accumulated points determine your position across three competitive timeframes:

| Leaderboard | Reset frequency | Best for |
|-------------|-----------------|----------|
| All-time | Never | Long-term achievement tracking |
| Monthly | First of each month | Medium-term competition |
| Weekly | Every Monday | Short-term goals and quick climbs |

## Tips for maximizing points

<Tip>
Prioritize accuracy over speed. A slower, more precise session typically earns more points than rushing through with poor form.
</Tip>

- **Train consistently**—regular sessions compound your point total over time
- **Rotate training modes**—variety prevents burnout and develops different skills
- **Share your progress**—community votes provide a passive point stream
- **Set incremental goals**—celebrate milestones to stay motivated
- **Study the leaderboards**—observe top performers for inspiration and technique ideas

<Note>
Every point represents real progress in your training journey. Focus on personal improvement, and the leaderboard rankings will follow.
</Note>
