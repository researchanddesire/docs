# Subscription

## **Our Commitment to You**

Your device, the dashboard and all their amazing core features are and will always be free to use! 🎉 We love creating technology that everyone can enjoy, and we’re allergic to the idea of charging a monthly fee to have fun.

That’s why we've designed our subscription model to focus on extras and customizations, but never to gate keep core functionality. We like to think of subscriptions as a way to enhance your experience while helping us dream up even more incredible features for everyone.

If you choose to subscribe, you’ll earn perks like special customizations, early access to new features and training modes, and the opportunity to contribute your ideas to the community. But you don’t need to subscribe, we’re just happy you’re here <3.

### Subscription Tiers

#### **Ultra Tier ⚡**

Love customization and being ahead of the curve? Ultra is for you! This fantastic tier includes:

- Show off your Ultra status with a sleek avatar flair
- Be among the first to try exciting beta features
- Make the platform truly yours with custom consent policies
- Take control of your AI training schedule
- Export your data just the way you want it
- Explore new and exciting training modes
- Up to 100 Deep Throat Trainer segments (instead of 3)
- Repeat segments up to 100 times to build longer, more efficient training templates
- Hands-Free Mode for continuous training sessions
- Pass/Fail mode options for segments

#### **Pioneer Tier 🚀**

Ready to help shape the future? Our Pioneer tier is perfect for enthusiasts who love being part of something bigger:

- Stand out with exclusive Pioneer avatar flair
- Get super-early access to alpha features
- Have your say in future developments through voting
- Enjoy all the perks of Ultra tier
- Be part of our growing innovation community

#### **Founder Tier 💫**

Want to be at the heart of our journey? The Founder tier puts you right in the middle of the action:

- Display the prestigious Founder avatar flair
- Play with experimental features hot from our lab
- Chat directly with our R\&D team twice a month
- Help guide our platform's future
- Access everything from Pioneer and Ultra tiers

### **Growing Together**

Your subscription does more than unlock cool features \* it helps us keep innovating and creating amazing experiences for everyone. We're so grateful for our subscribers who make it possible to keep developing new features while keeping core functionality free for all.
