## Create your account

<Steps>
<Step title="Visit the dashboard">
  Go to the [Research and Desire Dashboard](https://dashboard.researchanddesire.com) in your browser.
</Step>

<Step title="Start the sign-up process">
  Click **Create a free account** at the bottom of the login page.
</Step>

<Step title="Choose your sign-up method">
  Enter your email address and create a secure password, or sign up using one of the available social providers for faster access.
</Step>

<Step title="Verify your email">
  Check your inbox for a verification email and click the confirmation link to validate your address.

  <Tip>
  If you don't see the email, check your spam folder or request a new verification link.
  </Tip>
</Step>

<Step title="Complete onboarding">
  Follow the guided onboarding flow to configure your account and get started.

  <Check>
  Once onboarding is complete, you'll have full access to the dashboard.
  </Check>
</Step>
</Steps>
