After completing a training session, power off your device and remove the toy if desired. Then visit the [Research & Desire Dashboard](https://dashboard.researchanddesire.com) to review your performance.

## What you can track

On the dashboard, you can view:

- **Session grades** — See how you performed in each training session
- **Points earned** — Track your cumulative score over time
- **Leaderboard rankings** — Compare your progress with others in the community

<Tip>
Your partner can also log in to review these same details—perfect for accountability or encouragement as you climb the leaderboards.
</Tip>
