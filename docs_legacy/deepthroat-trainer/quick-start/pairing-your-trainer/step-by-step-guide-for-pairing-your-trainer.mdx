## Pairing Steps

<Steps>
<Step title="Power on your trainer">
  Ensure your battery is fully charged, then turn on your device. The trainer ID displays on screen during startup.
</Step>

<Step title="Find your trainer ID">
  Look at the display—you'll see **Trainer ID: XXXXX** in the top center. Write down this 5 or 6-character code.

  <Frame caption="Example trainer ID displayed on the device screen">
    <img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/IMG_5840.jpg" alt="Deepthroat Trainer screen showing the trainer ID location" width="375" />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Open the dashboard">
  Navigate to [dashboard.researchanddesire.com](https://dashboard.researchanddesire.com/) and sign in to your account.
</Step>

<Step title="Enter your trainer ID">
  Go to **Settings → Devices**, locate the "Deepthroat Trainer ID" field, and enter your code exactly as displayed on your device.
</Step>

<Step title="Complete pairing">
  Click **Pair Device** and you're all set. Your trainer is now connected to your account.

  <Check>
  You should see a confirmation message indicating successful pairing.
  </Check>
</Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
If you're transferring ownership of a previously paired device, the original owner must unpair it from their account first. Contact support if you need assistance with device transfers.
</Warning>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Forgot your trainer ID?">
Turn your device off and back on—the trainer ID displays again during startup.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Entered the wrong code?">
Double-check that you're entering the **trainer ID** (shown on boot), not the Wi-Fi password or any other code.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Device already paired to another account?">
Contact support to arrange a device ownership transfer. The current owner must unpair the device before you can pair it to your account.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
