### Quick Pairing Guide

Ready to unlock all the fun features? Here's the lowdown on getting your trainer connected to your account.

**Step 1: Find Your Trainer ID**\
Turn on your device and look for the trainer ID that appears on screen (it's a 5 or 6-character code). Write it down - you'll need it later! If you miss it, just power cycle your device and it'll show up again.

**Step 2: Connect to Your Account**\
Head over to the dashboard and pop that trainer ID into the settings. Boom - you're paired! Need the nitty-gritty details? Check out our pairing guide:
