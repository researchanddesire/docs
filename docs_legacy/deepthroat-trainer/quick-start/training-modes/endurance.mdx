Endurance mode focuses on building stamina and training your gag reflex. Your goal is to maintain position for the entire duration of your training session.

## How it works

1. Set your target duration before starting
2. Begin your session and maintain position
3. Coming up for air is allowed but affects your final grade
4. When the timer completes, your session ends and results upload to the dashboard

If you're using the vibrator attachment, it provides additional motivation to maintain position throughout the session.
