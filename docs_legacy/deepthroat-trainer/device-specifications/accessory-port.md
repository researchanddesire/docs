# Accessory Port

The Deepthroat Trainer has a 3.5mm jack used for connecting accessory devices.

Currently we sell vibrating plugs and wands for positive training reinforcement.

If you’d like to integrate this output into your own project, you can use the pinout diagram below.

The jack is a 4 conductor TRRS style 3.5mm. The first ring is used for the reward output, which is a 3.3V PWM signal that varies based on how well you are doing in a given training mode. It is capable of outputting 200mA of current.

When connecting other devices to the trainer, please take care to follow electrical safety precautions and local standards - no not connect the trainer directly to high voltages. We do not recommend connecting devices unless you are experienced and knowledgeable about the risks to yourself and the device - we will only officially support our R+D accessories. Any custom or 3rd party interfaces are at your own risk, so please be careful!
