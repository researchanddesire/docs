Your Deepthroat Trainer is designed to be straightforward to use and maintain. This section covers the technical specifications you need to know, from keeping your device charged to connecting accessories for enhanced training sessions.

## Hardware overview

The Deepthroat Trainer features:

- **USB-C charging port** for convenient, fast charging
- **3.5mm accessory port** for connecting vibrating accessories
- **Power switch** for easy on/off control
- **Built-in display** for real-time feedback
- **Front button** for interaction and control
