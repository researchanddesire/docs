# Charging Your Battery

### Charging and Turning On Your Deepthroat Trainer

The Trainer has a power switch on the side - sliding it up puts it in the ON position

When it is plugged into USB power it should indicate that it is charging on the screen. Holding the front button for 1 second will let you use the trainer while charging.

The battery lasts about 3-5 hours on a full charge depending on accessory usage.

If your device is not turning on, check these things:

1. **Check your power source.** Make sure your device is connected to a working outlet or USB port. If you're unsure, try plugging in another device to see if it receives power.
2. **Inspect your charging cable and adapter.** Look for any signs of damage, try using a different cable or adapter if you have one available.
3. **Leave your trainer to charge for a while.** Sometimes, the trainer's battery can be so depleted that it takes a while for it to have enough charge to power on. Leave your device connected to the charger for at least 30 minutes, then try turning it on again.
4. **Contact us!** If you've tried all the previous steps and your device still isn't turning on, it might be a hardware problem. Contact us and we’ll help you out!
