## Power Controls

Your Deepthroat Trainer has a power switch located on the side of the device. Slide it up to turn the device on.

<Tip>
You can use your Trainer while it's charging. Hold the front button for 1 second after plugging in to enable this mode.
</Tip>

## Charging Your Device

Connect a USB-C cable to the port on top of your Trainer. When connected to power, the screen displays the charging status.

A full charge provides approximately 3–5 hours of use, depending on accessory usage.

## Troubleshooting: Device Won't Turn On

If your Trainer isn't powering on, work through these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Verify your power source">
    Confirm your outlet or USB port is working by testing it with another device.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Inspect your charging cable and adapter">
    Check for visible damage on both the cable and adapter. If available, try a different USB-C cable or power adapter.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Allow time to charge">
    A deeply depleted battery may need time before the device can power on. Leave your Trainer connected to the charger for at least 30 minutes, then try turning it on again.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Contact support">
    If none of the above steps resolve the issue, you may have a hardware problem. Reach out to our support team for assistance.
  </Step>
</Steps>
