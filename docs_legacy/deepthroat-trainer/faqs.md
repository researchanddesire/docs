### What is the segment repeat feature and how do I use it?

The segment repeat feature is an Ultra-exclusive tool that allows you to set any training segment to repeat up to 100 times. Instead of manually adding the same segment over and over, simply set a repeat value on a single segment to create longer, more efficient training templates.

For example, if you want 10 repetitions of the same endurance segment, you can add one endurance segment and set its repeat value to 10. This counts as 10 effective segments toward your limit while keeping your template clean and easy to manage.

Free users are limited to 3 total effective segments (no repeats), while Ultra subscribers can use up to 100 total effective segments (including repeats). 💪

***

### What is Pass/Fail Mode and how does it work?

Pass/Fail Mode is an Ultra-exclusive feature that adds accountability to your training by requiring you to meet a minimum grade threshold for each segment.

When you enable Pass/Fail on a segment, you set a passing grade percentage (0-100%). If your performance meets or exceeds this threshold, you pass and move to the next segment. If you fall below it, the segment restarts and you must try again.

**The Three-Strike Rule:** If you fail the same segment three times in a row, your entire training session restarts from the beginning. This ensures you're truly mastering each segment before moving forward.

**Custom Failure Messages:** You can add personalized feedback (up to 80 characters) that displays on your device when you fail a segment, helping keep you motivated or reinforcing your training goals.

Pass/Fail can be enabled or disabled per segment when editing your training templates in the dashboard. It's perfect for building discipline and ensuring consistent performance! 💯

***

### What is Hands-Free Mode?

Hands-Free Mode is an Ultra-exclusive feature that allows your training to continue automatically without having to press the buttons on the trainer. When enabled on a training template, your Deepthroat Trainer will keep running through segments without requiring button clicks.

You can enable Hands-Free Mode when editing your training templates on the dashboard.

When using Hands-Free Mode, you will need to allow the timer to countdown fully before you begin your training segment- this should take about 10 secs.&#x20;

Only Ultra subscribers and above can enable this feature, and it must be activated on each training template individually.
