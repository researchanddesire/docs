Things don't always go as planned—and that's okay. This section helps you diagnose and resolve common issues quickly so you can get back to what matters.

## Before you begin

<Tip>
Most issues can be resolved by power cycling your device. Turn it off, wait 10 seconds, and turn it back on before diving into more complex troubleshooting.
</Tip>

## Common issues

Browse the guides in this section to find solutions for specific problems. Each guide walks you through the diagnostic steps and provides actionable fixes.

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Device won't turn on">
- Verify the battery is charged or the power supply is connected
- Check for any physical damage to the device or cables
- Try a different power outlet or USB port
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Connection problems">
- Ensure your device is within range
- Restart both the device and the app
- Check that Bluetooth or Wi-Fi is enabled on your phone
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="App not responding">
- Force close the app and reopen it
- Check for available app updates
- Restart your phone if the issue persists
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
